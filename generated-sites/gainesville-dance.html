Our Story

A Place to Move, Create & Grow

Gainesville Dance is a local dance school serving students of all ages and experience levels in the Gainesville community. Our studio offers a welcoming environment where beginners and experienced dancers alike can develop their skills, confidence, and passion for movement.

From classical styles to contemporary forms, our classes are designed to nurture creativity and technical growth in every student. Whether you are enrolling a child for the first time or returning to dance as an adult, there is a place for you here.

Located conveniently on NW 16th Blvd, we are proud to be part of the vibrant arts community in Gainesville, FL. Contact us for details on current class schedules and enrollment.
