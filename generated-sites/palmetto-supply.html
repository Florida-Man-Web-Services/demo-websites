About Us

Your Local Landscaping Supply Source

Palmetto Supply is a locally serving landscaping supply store in Gainesville, FL, providing homeowners, contractors, and landscapers with the bulk materials they need to bring outdoor spaces to life. From decorative stone and mulch to topsoil and fill, we carry a broad selection to cover every phase of your project.

We are committed to making it easy for the North Florida community to access the materials that matter — with knowledgeable staff ready to help you choose the right products for your specific landscape conditions and goals.

Whether you are tackling a weekend yard refresh or a large commercial install, contact us for details on availability and quantities.

Landscaping Supply Products & Services

Mulch & Wood Chips

A variety of mulch types to suppress weeds, retain moisture, and give planting beds a clean, finished look. Contact us for details on available styles and colors.

Topsoil & Fill Dirt

Premium topsoil and fill options to level, grade, or build up planting areas. Available in bulk quantities suitable for both residential and commercial applications.
