About Us

Veterinary Care On Your Terms

Mobile Pet Doctor brings professional veterinary services directly to your home in Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida area. We understand that traveling to a clinic can be stressful for both pets and their owners, and our mobile practice is built to change that experience entirely.

Our team is dedicated to providing thorough, attentive care in the familiar surroundings where your pet feels most at ease. From routine wellness visits to more targeted medical needs, we bring the clinic to your doorstep with the same standard of care you would expect in a traditional setting.

Serving the Gainesville community, we are committed to making quality veterinary care more accessible and less anxiety-inducing for every patient we see. Contact us for details on availability and service areas.

What We Offer

Our Services

⚕️

Wellness Exams

Comprehensive head-to-tail physical examinations to assess your pet's overall health and catch any concerns early.

💉

Vaccinations

Core and lifestyle vaccinations administered at home to keep your pet protected according to current veterinary guidelines.

🔬

Diagnostics

In-home diagnostic services including bloodwork collection and other assessments to help evaluate your pet's health status.

💊

Parasite Prevention & Treatment

Recommendations and treatments for fleas, ticks, heartworm, and other parasites tailored to your pet's lifestyle and needs.

🐾

Sick Pet Visits

When your pet isn't feeling well, we come to you to evaluate symptoms and provide appropriate care and guidance.

📋

Senior Pet Care

Specialized attention for older pets who may benefit most from the comfort and reduced stress of receiving care at home.
