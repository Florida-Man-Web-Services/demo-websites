About Us

A Local Shop You Can Actually Trust

Tommy's Autoworks is a full-service auto repair shop serving the Gainesville community with straightforward, no-nonsense vehicle care. We believe every driver deserves honest assessments and repairs done right the first time — no upsells, no surprises.

From daily commuters to work trucks, our certified technicians handle a wide range of makes and models. We take the time to explain what your car needs and why, so you leave feeling confident, not confused.

Located conveniently on NE 23rd Ave in Gainesville, we're your neighborhood shop for everything from routine maintenance to more involved mechanical work. Contact us for current availability and details.
