About Us

A Nail Experience Worth Savoring

Nestled in northwest Gainesville, 39 Nail Salon is a welcoming destination for clients who appreciate quality nail care in a calm, clean, and stylish environment. From classic manicures to intricate nail art, every service is delivered with precision and attention to detail.

We are committed to using high-quality products and maintaining the highest sanitation standards so that every visit feels both luxurious and worry-free. Whether you are treating yourself or getting ready for a special occasion, our team is here to make you feel your best.
