About Us

Detail-Driven. Results-Focused.

Main Street Detailing is a locally operated auto detailing service serving Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida community. We take pride in delivering thorough, attentive care for every vehicle that comes through our doors — from daily drivers to weekend classics.

Our team uses professional-grade products and proven techniques to clean, polish, and protect your vehicle's interior and exterior surfaces. Whether you're looking to refresh your daily commuter or prepare a vehicle for a special occasion, we bring the same level of dedication to every job.

Conveniently located on N Main St, we make it easy to get the detail your car deserves. Contact us to learn more about what we offer or to schedule an appointment.
