Our Story

Authentic Korean Cuisine in Gainesville

K-INACUP brings the rich, layered flavors of Korean cuisine to downtown Gainesville, serving up everything from hearty bibimbap and savory Korean fried chicken to comforting soups and traditional small-plate sides known as banchan.

Nestled on SW 2nd Ave, the restaurant offers a welcoming atmosphere for students, families, and anyone curious about the depth and variety of Korean cooking — from mild and aromatic to boldly spiced.

Whether you are stopping in for a quick lunch or settling in for a full dinner experience, K-INACUP is committed to quality ingredients and dishes prepared with care. Contact us for current hours and seasonal specials.

What We Offer

Menu Highlights

A curated selection of traditional and contemporary Korean dishes made fresh daily. Contact us for the current menu and seasonal items.

🍚

Rice & Bibimbap Bowls

Classic Korean mixed rice bowls topped with seasoned vegetables, protein, and gochujang sauce — a satisfying staple of Korean home cooking.

🍗

Korean Fried Chicken

Crispy double-fried chicken tossed in your choice of sauces, from soy-garlic to spicy yangnyeom — perfect as a meal or a shareable snack.

🍜

Korean Soups & Stews

Warming broths and hearty jjigae stews, including options like kimchi jjigae and doenjang jjigae, served bubbling hot with steamed rice.

🥟

Dumplings & Appetizers

Handcrafted mandu dumplings and savory pancakes like pajeon make for ideal starters or light bites alongside any main dish.

🥗

Banchan (Side Dishes)

An assortment of traditional Korean small plates — including kimchi, seasoned spinach, and pickled vegetables — served alongside your meal.

🧋

Korean Beverages & Desserts

Round out your meal with Korean-inspired drinks and sweet treats. Contact us for details on current beverage and dessert offerings.
