About Us

Hardwood Flooring Done Right

Tucker Brothers Wood Floor Specialist Inc has been serving homeowners and businesses across the Gainesville area with skilled hardwood floor installation, refinishing, and restoration. We take pride in delivering durable, beautiful results that enhance any space.

Whether you are transforming a single room or an entire property, our team brings hands-on expertise and an eye for detail to every project. We work with a wide variety of wood species, finishes, and styles to match your vision and your budget.

Locally rooted in Gainesville, we understand the needs of Florida homes and commercial spaces. Contact us to discuss your flooring project and get started.

What We Do

Our Services

From brand-new installs to full restorations, Tucker Brothers handles all aspects of wood flooring. Contact us for details on pricing and scheduling.

Hardwood Floor Installation

New solid or engineered hardwood installation for residential and commercial spaces. We help you select the right species, grade, and layout for lasting beauty.

Floor Sanding & Refinishing

Restore worn or scratched hardwood floors to like-new condition. Professional sanding and refinishing removes damage and applies a fresh protective coat.

Floor Repair & Restoration

Targeted repairs for squeaky boards, gaps, cupping, or water-damaged sections. We match existing wood and finish for a seamless, uniform appearance.

Staining & Finishing

Custom stain colors and finish options including oil-based, water-based, and matte to glossy sheens. We help you find the perfect look for your space.

Subfloor Preparation

Proper subfloor leveling and preparation before installation. A solid, flat foundation ensures your new hardwood floors perform and look their best for years.

Free Estimates

Not sure where to start? We offer free on-site consultations and estimates for all flooring projects. Contact us for details and to schedule your appointment.
