What We Offer

Our Cleaning Services

From routine upkeep to intensive deep cleans, we offer a range of services to keep your home looking its best.

Standard House Cleaning

A thorough top-to-bottom clean of your home's main living areas, bedrooms, bathrooms, and kitchen — ideal for regular upkeep.

Deep Cleaning

An intensive cleaning that reaches areas often missed in standard visits — behind appliances, inside cabinets, grout lines, baseboards, and more.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Specialized cleaning for properties being vacated or freshly occupied, ensuring the space is spotless for its next chapter.

Kitchen & Appliance Cleaning

Detailed attention to stovetops, ovens, microwaves, refrigerator exteriors, sinks, and countertops to keep your kitchen hygienic.

Bathroom Sanitization

Thorough disinfecting of toilets, showers, tubs, and sinks, plus mirror polishing and floor scrubbing for a fresh, germ-free bathroom.

Recurring Maintenance Cleaning

Scheduled weekly, biweekly, or monthly cleans to keep your home consistently maintained — contact us to set up a recurring plan.
