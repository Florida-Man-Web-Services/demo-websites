About Us

Built for Builders

Contractors Supply is a locally serving building materials store in Gainesville, FL, stocking a wide selection of construction supplies, hardware, and specialty products for professionals and homeowners alike.

Located conveniently on NE Waldo Road, we make it easy for contractors, remodelers, and serious DIYers to find what they need to get the job done right — without the hassle of big-box hunting.

Whether you are framing a new structure, finishing an interior, or sourcing specific hardware, our knowledgeable team is here to help. Contact us for pricing, availability, and project guidance.
