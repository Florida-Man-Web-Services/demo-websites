About Us

A Place Where Every Child Belongs

🎨📚🧩 🌈🌻🎵 🚀🦋🌟

Elevate Early Learning Academy is a dedicated child care center serving families throughout Gainesville, FL. Our team of caring educators provides a safe, stimulating space where children build the foundational skills that set them up for lifelong success.

We believe that play is a child's most important work. Our curriculum blends structured learning with creative exploration, giving each child the individual attention they deserve in a warm, home-like setting.

From infants to school-age children, we partner closely with families, offering open communication and a genuine commitment to each little learner's well-being and development.
