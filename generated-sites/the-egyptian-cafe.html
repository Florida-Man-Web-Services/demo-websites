Our Story

A Corner of Egypt in Gainesville

The Egyptian Cafe brings the rich culinary traditions of the Middle East and North Africa to downtown Gainesville, offering a welcoming space where students, locals, and visitors can slow down and savor something extraordinary.

From aromatic coffees and freshly brewed loose-leaf teas to hearty savory dishes and sweet pastries, every item on the menu reflects a deep respect for authentic Egyptian flavors and warm hospitality.

Whether you are stopping in for a quiet afternoon coffee or sharing a meal with friends, The Egyptian Cafe offers a genuinely distinctive dining experience unlike anything else in Gainesville.
