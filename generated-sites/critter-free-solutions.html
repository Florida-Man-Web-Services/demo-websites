About Us

Your Trusted Pest Control Team in Gainesville

Critter-Free Solutions is a Gainesville-based pest control company dedicated to keeping homes and businesses free from unwanted insects, rodents, and wildlife throughout Alachua County and the surrounding region.

Our technicians use proven, targeted treatment methods designed to eliminate pest problems at the source while minimizing impact on your family, pets, and the environment. Whether it's a routine inspection or an urgent infestation, we respond promptly and treat every property with care.

From termite prevention to wildlife exclusion, we offer a full range of residential and commercial pest management services tailored to the unique conditions of North Central Florida. Contact us to find out which treatment plan is right for you.

What We Do

Our Pest Control Services

General Pest Control

Comprehensive treatment for common household pests including ants, roaches, spiders, silverfish, and more. Contact us for details on scheduling and treatment plans.

Termite Control & Prevention

Inspection, treatment, and ongoing prevention programs to protect your property from subterranean and drywood termites common in North Central Florida.

Rodent Exclusion & Removal

Identification of entry points, humane trapping, and structural exclusion to keep mice and rats out of your home or business for good.

Mosquito & Tick Treatment

Barrier treatments and yard management programs to significantly reduce mosquito and tick populations around your outdoor living spaces. Contact us for details.

Wildlife Removal & Exclusion

Humane removal and relocation of nuisance wildlife including squirrels, raccoons, opossums, and other animals that have entered your structure.

Commercial Pest Management

Customized integrated pest management (IPM) programs for restaurants, offices, warehouses, and multi-unit properties to maintain compliance and protect your reputation.
