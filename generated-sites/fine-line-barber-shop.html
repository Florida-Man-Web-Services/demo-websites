The Shop

A neighborhood barber shop on Gainesville's northeast side, built on steady hands and sharp lines.

Fine Line Barber Shop is a Gainesville spot for people who care about the details — the clean edge along the temple, the seamless blend of a fade, the crisp line that finishes the look. Every cut gets full attention from start to finish.

The difference is in the line — where the cut ends and the craft begins.

Whether you're a regular, a walk-in, or trying us for the first time, you'll find a relaxed chair and a barber who takes pride in the work. From tight fades to beard shaping, the goal is simple: you leave looking sharp.

Located at 1152 NE 16th Ave, Fine Line is a short trip for students, professionals, and families across Gainesville. Contact us for current availability and scheduling.
