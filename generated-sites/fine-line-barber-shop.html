Our Story

Where Every Cut
Is a Fine Line

Fine Line Barber Shop is a cornerstone of the Gainesville community, offering sharp, precise cuts in a relaxed and welcoming environment. Whether you are a student at UF, a longtime local, or just passing through, you will find a chair that feels like home.

Our experienced barbers take pride in their craft — blending traditional barbering techniques with contemporary styles to give every client a look that is clean, confident, and distinctly their own.

We believe a great haircut is about more than appearance. It is about the experience — the conversation, the attention to detail, and the moment you look in the mirror and feel your best. Contact us for details on scheduling and availability.
