Who We Are

Your Neighborhood Tire Shop in Gainesville

Low Cost Tires LLC is a used tire shop serving drivers throughout Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida area. We specialize in providing quality pre-owned tires at prices that fit real budgets, so you never have to choose between safety and affordability.

Whether you need a single replacement tire or a full set, our team inspects every tire for safety and reliability before it hits our lot. We carry a wide variety of sizes to fit cars, trucks, SUVs, and more.

Located on S Main St, we are easy to find and ready to help — no appointment necessary. Contact us for current inventory and pricing details.

Our Services

Used Tire Sales

Wide selection of quality pre-owned tires in various sizes to fit cars, trucks, SUVs, and vans. Contact us for current inventory.

Tire Installation

Professional mounting and installation of tires on your vehicle. Fast service to get you back on the road quickly.

Tire Balancing

Proper tire balancing to reduce vibration, improve ride comfort, and extend the life of your tires and suspension components.

Tire Rotation

Regular tire rotation service to promote even tread wear across all four tires, helping maximize their usable life.

Flat Tire Assistance

Caught a flat? Stop in and we will assess the damage and get you set up with a suitable replacement tire fast.

Tire Inspection & Pressure Check

Quick visual inspection and pressure check to ensure your tires are safe and properly inflated before you head out.
