A Neighborhood Cafe Worth Knowing

108 Vine is a beloved neighborhood coffee shop nestled along West University Avenue, offering Gainesville a welcoming retreat from the everyday hustle. With an emphasis on thoughtfully prepared coffee and a relaxed, unhurried atmosphere, it has become a go-to spot for students, locals, and anyone who appreciates a genuinely great cup.

The space strikes a balance between cozy and creative — the kind of place you come in for a quick espresso and end up staying for the afternoon. Whether you're settling in with a laptop, meeting a friend, or simply savoring something warm, 108 Vine feels like it was made for exactly that moment.

Committed to quality and community, 108 Vine continues to be one of Gainesville's most cherished independent coffee destinations, backed by a loyal following and a near-perfect Google rating.
