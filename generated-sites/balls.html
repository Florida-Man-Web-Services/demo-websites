About Us

A Bar Built for Gainesville

Balls is a neighborhood bar at the heart of Gainesville's lively University Avenue corridor, serving students, locals, and anyone looking for a genuinely good time in a laid-back atmosphere. Whether you're catching a game, unwinding after a long week, or just looking for a cold drink and good company, we've got a spot for you. With a welcoming crowd and a no-frills vibe that keeps people coming back, we've become a staple of the local bar scene.
