About Us

A Community Built on Tradition

Moring-San Martial Arts is a dedicated training center serving the Gainesville community, offering structured martial arts instruction rooted in time-honored principles of respect, perseverance, and self-improvement. Our programs welcome students of all ages and experience levels, from complete beginners to seasoned practitioners seeking to refine their skills.

Whether you are looking to improve fitness, learn self-defense, or grow personally through disciplined practice, our instructors provide a focused and supportive environment to help every student reach their goals.

Our Programs

Classes for Every Level

We offer a wide range of martial arts disciplines and class formats. Contact us for current scheduling and enrollment details.

🥋

Beginner Foundations

A welcoming introduction to martial arts covering basic stances, strikes, and etiquette. Perfect for those stepping onto the mat for the first time.

🏳️

Belt Ranking & Advancement

Structured curriculum guiding students through progressive belt levels, with testing and recognition of achievement at every stage of their journey.

💪

Self-Defense Training

Practical, real-world self-defense techniques designed to build awareness, confidence, and the ability to protect yourself in everyday situations.

👦

Youth & Kids Classes

Age-appropriate martial arts instruction that builds focus, respect, and physical coordination in children while keeping sessions engaging and fun.

🏃

Fitness & Conditioning

High-energy training sessions combining martial arts fundamentals with cardio and strength work to improve overall physical fitness and endurance.

Advanced & Sparring

Competitive and advanced training for experienced students, focusing on technique refinement, sparring strategy, and tournament preparation.
