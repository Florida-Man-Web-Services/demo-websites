Who We Are

A Community-Focused Clothing Destination

Outreach Clothing & More Center is a welcoming retail destination in Gainesville, FL, offering a curated selection of clothing and everyday essentials for men, women, and children of all backgrounds.

Whether you're refreshing your wardrobe or stocking up on everyday basics, Outreach Clothing & More Center is here to serve the Gainesville community with care and respect.
