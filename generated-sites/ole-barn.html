Our Story

A Gainesville
Neighborhood Bar

Ole Barn is a laid-back neighborhood bar on the northeast side of Gainesville where locals come to unwind, catch the game, and share a round with familiar faces.

The atmosphere is easygoing and unpretentious — exactly what a good bar should be. Whether you're stopping in for a cold beer after work or settling in for an evening with friends, Ole Barn delivers the kind of relaxed, welcoming vibe that keeps regulars coming back week after week.

Situated at 1791 NE 23rd Ave, the bar is a convenient gathering spot for Gainesville residents looking for somewhere comfortable to kick back without the fuss.
