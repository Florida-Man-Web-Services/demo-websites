About Us

A Trusted Accounting Partner in Gainesville

Mink & Agarwal, PLLC is a full-service accounting firm located in Gainesville, FL, dedicated to providing accurate, reliable, and confidential financial services to our clients across Alachua County and beyond.

Our team of certified professionals takes the time to understand each client's unique financial situation, whether you're an individual navigating tax season or a local business in need of ongoing bookkeeping and compliance support.

We are committed to building long-term relationships built on trust, transparency, and a thorough understanding of both state and federal tax obligations. Contact us for details about how we can best serve your needs.

Our Services

Comprehensive accounting and financial services for individuals, families, and businesses throughout Gainesville and North Central Florida.

Tax Preparation & Filing
