About Us

A Trusted Name in Watch Repair

Time Connection is Gainesville's dedicated watch repair service, offering careful, skilled attention to timepieces of all makes and styles. Whether you own a cherished heirloom or an everyday watch, we treat every repair with the same level of craftsmanship and respect for the piece.

Our focus is on providing honest assessments and quality workmanship so your watch leaves in better shape than when it arrived. We welcome walk-ins and are happy to answer any questions about your timepiece's condition.

Located conveniently in northeast Gainesville, we serve customers throughout the area looking for reliable, professional watch care close to home.
