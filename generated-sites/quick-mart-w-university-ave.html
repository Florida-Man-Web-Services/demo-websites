About Us

Your Go-To Stop on University Ave

Quick Mart on W University Ave is a convenient neighborhood stop serving the Gainesville community with everyday essentials, snacks, beverages, and more. Whether you're a student, a local resident, or just passing through, we aim to make your quick stop as easy and efficient as possible.

Conveniently located at 1800 W University Ave, we stock a wide selection of products so you can grab what you need and get on with your day. Our friendly staff is ready to help — no matter how big or small your request.

From cold drinks to household basics, we keep our shelves well-stocked so the things you reach for most are always within easy reach.
