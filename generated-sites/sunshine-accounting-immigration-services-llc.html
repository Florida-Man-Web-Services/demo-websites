About Us

Comprehensive Support for Your Financial & Immigration Needs

Sunshine Accounting & Immigration Services, LLC serves the Gainesville community with professional accounting, tax preparation, and immigration assistance — all under one roof. Our team understands the unique needs of individuals, families, and small businesses navigating both financial and immigration matters.

We pride ourselves on offering personalized, attentive service and clear guidance through complex processes. Whether you need help with your taxes, bookkeeping, or an immigration petition, we work to make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.
