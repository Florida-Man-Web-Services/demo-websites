About Us

Reliable Repairs for Every Vehicle

Advance Auto Repair & A/C LLC is a full-service automotive shop located right on East University Ave in Gainesville, FL — conveniently serving University of Florida students, staff, and the surrounding community.

Our certified technicians are equipped to handle everything from oil changes and brake service to complex engine diagnostics and air conditioning repairs, ensuring your vehicle is road-ready year-round in Florida's demanding climate.

We pride ourselves on honest, straightforward service with no unnecessary upsells — just quality work at fair prices. Contact us for details on any service.

What We Do

Our Services

A/C Repair & Service

Recharge, leak detection, compressor replacement, and full air conditioning system diagnostics to keep your cabin cool in Florida's heat.

Oil Change & Lube

Conventional and synthetic oil changes with filter replacement and multi-point inspection to extend engine life and maintain warranty requirements.

Brake Inspection & Repair

Pad and rotor replacement, caliper service, and brake fluid flushes to ensure safe, reliable stopping power on every drive.

Engine Diagnostics

Computer-assisted check-engine light diagnostics pinpoint issues fast, saving time and preventing minor problems from becoming costly repairs.

Transmission Service

Fluid flushes, filter changes, and transmission inspections to keep your drivetrain shifting smoothly and prevent premature wear.

Battery & Electrical

Battery testing, replacement, alternator and starter inspection, and electrical system troubleshooting to prevent unexpected breakdowns.
