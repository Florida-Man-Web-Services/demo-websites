About Us

Local Electrical Expertise You Can Trust

What We Do

Electrical Services

We handle a wide range of electrical work for homes and businesses. Contact us for details on pricing and availability.

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

Upgrade an outdated or undersized electrical panel to safely meet the demands of modern appliances, EV chargers, and home additions.

Wiring & Rewiring

New construction wiring, whole-home rewiring, and circuit additions for renovations — all done to code and properly inspected.

Outlet & Switch Installation

Installation or replacement of standard outlets, GFCI outlets, USB outlets, smart switches, dimmers, and specialty receptacles.

Electrical Troubleshooting & Repair

Diagnosis and repair of tripping breakers, flickering lights, dead outlets, and other electrical faults to restore safe operation.

Ceiling Fan & Lighting Installation

Installation of ceiling fans, recessed lighting, outdoor fixtures, and whole-home lighting upgrades including LED conversions.

Surge Protection & Safety Inspections

Whole-home surge protection installation and electrical safety inspections to protect your investment and identify potential hazards.
