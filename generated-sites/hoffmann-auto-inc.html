About Us

Your Local Auto Repair Specialists

Hoffmann Auto Inc is a full-service auto repair shop serving the Gainesville, FL community and surrounding areas. Our skilled technicians are committed to providing reliable, high-quality repairs and maintenance you can count on.

We work on a wide range of makes and models, using quality parts and up-to-date diagnostic equipment to get your vehicle running right. Whether it's a routine oil change or a complex repair, we treat every car like our own.

Transparency and trust are at the heart of everything we do. We'll explain what your vehicle needs before we begin, so you always know what to expect. Contact us for details on scheduling and current availability.

What We Do

Full-Service Auto Repair & Maintenance

From routine upkeep to complex repairs, our team handles it all. Contact us for details on availability and scheduling.

Oil Change & Fluid Service

Regular oil changes and fluid top-offs to keep your engine protected and running efficiently. Contact us for details on service intervals.

Brake Inspection & Repair

Full brake system inspections, pad and rotor replacement, and brake fluid service to ensure safe stopping performance.

Engine Diagnostics

Advanced computer diagnostics to pinpoint check-engine lights, fault codes, and performance issues accurately before any repair begins.

Tire Services

Tire rotation, balancing, and inflation checks to extend tire life and improve fuel economy and handling on Gainesville roads.
