Our Mission

Dedicated to Ending Pet Homelessness in Gainesville

No More Homeless Pets, Inc is a community-driven non-profit organization committed to reducing the number of homeless animals in Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida region through adoption, spay/neuter programs, and public education.

We partner with local shelters, rescue groups, and caring volunteers to provide animals with the care and placement they need, while helping families find their perfect companion.

Our work is made possible by the generosity of donors, adopters, and dedicated volunteers who believe that every animal deserves a safe and loving home.

What We Do

Programs & Services

We offer a range of programs to support animals in need and the community members who love them. Contact us for details on availability and eligibility.

🐱

Pet Adoption

Connecting dogs, cats, and small animals with caring families in the Gainesville area through our adoption placement program.

✂

Spay & Neuter Assistance

Helping reduce pet overpopulation by supporting access to low-cost or subsidized spay and neuter services for community pets.

💊

Foster Program

Recruiting and supporting foster families who provide temporary loving care for animals awaiting permanent placement.

📚

Community Education

Offering educational outreach on responsible pet ownership, humane treatment, and the importance of spay/neuter programs.

🤖

Volunteer Opportunities

Engaging community members through meaningful volunteer roles that directly support our animals and organizational mission.

💌

Donations & Fundraising

Accepting monetary donations, supplies, and in-kind gifts to sustain our programs and expand our reach across the community.
