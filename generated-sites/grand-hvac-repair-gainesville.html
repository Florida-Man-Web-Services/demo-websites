About Us

Gainesville's HVAC Specialists

Grand HVAC Repair Gainesville provides dependable heating, ventilation, and air conditioning services to homeowners throughout the Gainesville area. North Central Florida's climate demands systems that perform flawlessly — from scorching summer months to cooler winter spells — and our team is equipped to keep yours running at peak efficiency.

We work on all major HVAC brands and system types, offering honest assessments and straightforward repairs so you know exactly what you're getting. Whether you need a quick fix, a full system replacement, or a routine tune-up, we're committed to getting the job done right the first time.

Located right here in Gainesville at 816 SE 4th Ave, we take pride in being a locally focused contractor you can reach quickly when your system needs attention. Contact us for details on scheduling and availability.

What We Do

Our Services

From emergency repairs to preventive maintenance, we cover the full range of residential HVAC needs across Gainesville and the surrounding communities.

AC Repair

Diagnosing and fixing air conditioning problems large and small — from refrigerant leaks and compressor issues to thermostat malfunctions and restricted airflow.

Heating System Repair

Keeping your heating system reliable during cooler months, including heat pump service, electric furnace repair, and general heating system diagnostics.

HVAC Installation

New system installation for homes upgrading outdated equipment or building out new spaces, sized and configured to match your home's specific needs.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled tune-ups and inspections to extend equipment life, improve energy efficiency, and catch small issues before they become expensive repairs.

System Inspections

Thorough inspections of all HVAC components — coils, filters, ductwork, electrical connections, and refrigerant levels — with a clear report of findings.

Ductwork & Airflow

Assessment and repair of ductwork leaks, blockages, and design issues that cause uneven cooling, poor air quality, or reduced system efficiency.
