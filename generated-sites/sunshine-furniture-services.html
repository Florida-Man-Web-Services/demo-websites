About Us

Trusted Furniture Repair in Gainesville

Sunshine Furniture Services is a locally owned furniture repair shop proudly serving the Gainesville community. We specialize in restoring the beauty and functionality of all types of furniture — from antique heirlooms to modern everyday pieces.

Our skilled craftsmen take pride in careful, detail-oriented work, using quality materials and proven techniques to deliver results that last. Whether a chair needs reupholstering, a wood surface needs refinishing, or a structural repair is required, we handle each job with the same dedication.

What We Do

Our Furniture Repair Services

From structural fixes to full cosmetic overhauls, we offer a comprehensive range of furniture repair and restoration services.

Structural Repair

Broken legs, loose joints, cracked frames — we restore the structural integrity of chairs, tables, beds, and more so your furniture is safe and sturdy again.

Wood Refinishing

Scratches, stains, fading, and worn finishes disappear with our professional wood refinishing and stripping service, revealing the natural beauty of your wood furniture.

Reupholstering

Give worn or dated fabric new life. We reupholster sofas, dining chairs, ottomans, and other upholstered pieces with a wide selection of fabrics. Contact us for available options.

Stain & Water Damage Repair

Water rings, heat marks, and deep stains can often be treated and minimized. We assess and address surface damage to restore your furniture's appearance.

Antique Restoration

Heirloom and antique pieces require a careful touch. We work to preserve the character and original details of your valuable antiques while making necessary repairs.

Touch-Up & Finishing

Minor chips, dings, and color mismatches handled with precision blending and touch-up techniques to seamlessly restore the look of your furniture's surface.
