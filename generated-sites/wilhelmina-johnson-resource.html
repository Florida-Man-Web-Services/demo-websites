About Us

Serving the Gainesville Community

The Wilhelmina Johnson Resource office is a dedicated city government facility committed to providing Gainesville residents with access to civic programs, public information, and community assistance resources. Our office serves as a central point of contact for residents seeking support from local government services.

Located in the heart of Gainesville, we work to ensure every member of the community has equal access to the public resources and programs they need. We are committed to responsive, transparent, and respectful public service for all.

What We Offer

Community Services & Resources

Our office provides a range of civic services and community resources to Gainesville residents. Contact us for eligibility details, availability, and additional information.

Public Records & Information

Assistance accessing public documents, government records, and official city information in accordance with Florida public records law.

Community Assistance Programs

Referrals and enrollment support for local assistance programs designed to help Gainesville residents in need of community resources.

Resident Registration & Enrollment

Support with registration processes for city programs, services, and civic participation opportunities available to Gainesville residents.

Civic Program Coordination

Information and coordination support for city-sponsored civic programs, public events, and community engagement initiatives.

Constituent Services & Inquiries

A direct point of contact for residents with questions, concerns, or feedback related to city government services and public programs.

Local Resource Referrals

Guidance and referrals to partner agencies, community organizations, and other city departments that can assist Gainesville residents.
