About Us

Your Trusted Used Tire Shop in Gainesville

Sam's Tires Of Gainesville is a locally operated used tire shop serving drivers throughout the Gainesville area. We carry a wide selection of quality pre-owned tires for all vehicle makes and models, inspected to meet safety standards before every sale.

Whether you need a single replacement tire or a full set, our knowledgeable staff works quickly to get you rolling again at a price that fits your budget. We take pride in providing straightforward, honest service to every customer who pulls into our shop.

Conveniently located on NW 6th St, we serve customers from Gainesville and surrounding communities. Contact us for current availability and pricing.

What We Do

Our Services

Used Tire Sales

A large inventory of quality pre-owned tires inspected for safety, available in a wide range of sizes for cars, trucks, and SUVs.

Tire Mounting

Professional mounting of tires onto your wheels, ensuring a secure fit and proper bead seating for safe driving.

Tire Balancing

Wheel balancing service to reduce vibration, prevent uneven wear, and improve the ride quality and handling of your vehicle.

Tire Rotation

Regular tire rotation helps extend the life of your tires by promoting even tread wear across all four positions.

Flat Tire Repair

Quick assessment and repair of repairable punctures and flats so you can get back on the road without buying a replacement.

Affordable Pricing

Competitive, budget-friendly pricing on all used tires and services. Contact us for current availability and quotes.
