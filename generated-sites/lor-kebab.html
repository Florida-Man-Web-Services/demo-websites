Our Story

A Taste of Persian Tradition

Lor Kebab brings the rich culinary heritage of Persia to the heart of downtown Gainesville. Every dish is crafted with time-honored spice blends and slow-cooked techniques that honor the depth and warmth of Iranian home cooking.

From fragrant rice dishes layered with saffron to charcoal-grilled skewers marinated overnight, our kitchen is devoted to authenticity and freshness. We source quality ingredients and prepare each plate with the care it deserves.

Whether you are a longtime lover of Persian food or discovering it for the first time, Lor Kebab offers a welcoming table and a menu that speaks for itself. Contact us for current availability and details.

What We Offer

Menu Highlights

🍖

Grilled Kebabs

Tender skewers of seasoned lamb, beef, and chicken, charcoal-grilled to perfection and served with saffron rice and grilled tomatoes.

🍲

Persian Stews (Khoresht)

Slow-simmered stews such as ghormeh sabzi and fesenjan, rich with herbs, pomegranate, and warming spices — the heart of Persian cuisine.

🍚

Saffron Rice Dishes

Fluffy basmati rice preparations including chelo and polo varieties, infused with golden saffron and topped with a crispy tahdig crust.

🥫

Mezze & Appetizers

A selection of Persian starters including hummus, kashk-e bademjan (eggplant dip), fresh herbs, and warm flatbread.

🧅

Soups & Ash

Hearty Persian soups like ash reshteh — thick, herb-laden, and deeply nourishing, served as a traditional starter or standalone meal.

🍮

Persian Desserts & Drinks

Finish with rosewater-scented sweets, baklava, or a refreshing glass of doogh. Dessert selections vary — contact us for current offerings.
