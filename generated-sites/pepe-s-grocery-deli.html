About Us

A Familiar Market in the Heart of Gainesville

Pepe's Grocery & Deli is a neighborhood grocery store serving the southwest Gainesville community with a full selection of fresh produce, pantry staples, and ready-to-eat deli items. We strive to make everyday shopping convenient and welcoming for families, students, and longtime residents alike.

Our deli counter is stocked with freshly prepared options to make mealtime easy, while our grocery aisles carry a thoughtful mix of everyday essentials and specialty goods. Whether you are stopping in for a quick lunch or a full weekly shop, you will find what you need at Pepe's.

Located on SW 42nd Dr, we are proud to be a convenient, community-focused market where friendly service and quality selection come together.
