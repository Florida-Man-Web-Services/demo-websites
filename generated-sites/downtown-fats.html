About Us

The Heartbeat of Downtown Gainesville

“Where the night comes alive in the heart of Gainesville”

Downtown Fats is a premier nightlife destination sitting right at the heart of Gainesville’s vibrant downtown scene. Located on South Main Street, our venue blends a sleek, high-energy atmosphere with top-tier sound and lighting, making every visit feel like an event.


We cater to Gainesville’s vibrant crowd — students, locals, and visitors alike — and take pride in curating memorable nights that keep people coming back. Steps from the best dining and entertainment the city has to offer, Downtown Fats is your go-to when the night calls.
