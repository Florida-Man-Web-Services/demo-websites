About Us

Craftsmanship You Can Feel

Steve's Headliners is a locally owned auto upholstery shop serving the Gainesville, FL community. We specialize in repairing, replacing, and restoring all types of automotive interior surfaces to bring your vehicle back to its best condition.

From sagging headliners to worn seat upholstery, our skilled craftsmen use quality materials and proven techniques to deliver results that look factory-fresh and feel built to last.

Whether your car, truck, or SUV needs a minor repair or a complete interior overhaul, we take pride in every job we do. Contact us today to discuss your vehicle's needs.
