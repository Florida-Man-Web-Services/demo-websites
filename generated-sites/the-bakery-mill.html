A Neighborhood Bakery Worth Visiting

Northwest Gainesville

The Bakery Mill is a beloved neighborhood bakery in northwest Gainesville, dedicated to bringing the community fresh, scratch-made baked goods every day. From rustic sourdough loaves to delicate pastries, everything that comes out of our ovens is crafted with quality ingredients and old-fashioned care.

Whether you're stopping in for your morning coffee and a croissant or ordering a custom cake for a special occasion, we strive to make every visit feel warm and welcoming. We're proud to serve the Gainesville community and look forward to sharing a little sweetness with you.
