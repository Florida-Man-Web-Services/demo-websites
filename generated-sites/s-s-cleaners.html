About Us

Cleaning You Can Count On

S & S Cleaners is a trusted dry cleaning and garment care destination serving the Gainesville community. We combine professional-grade cleaning techniques with careful attention to each item we handle, from everyday work attire to special occasion garments.

Whether you need a suit pressed for an important meeting or delicate fabrics treated with extra care, our team is committed to returning your clothes in pristine condition. We take pride in the quality of every garment we touch.

Conveniently located on SW 3rd St in the heart of Gainesville, we make it easy to drop off and pick up on your schedule. Contact us for details on hours and turnaround times.

What We Offer

Our Services

Dry Cleaning

Professional solvent-based cleaning for suits, dress shirts, dresses, and garments that require extra care beyond standard laundering.

Laundry & Press

Wash, dry, and press services for everyday clothing, dress shirts, and blouses, returned neatly folded or on hangers.

Alterations & Tailoring

Expert fitting adjustments including hemming, taking in or letting out seams, and other garment modifications to ensure the perfect fit.

Wedding Gown Care

Specialized cleaning and preservation for bridal gowns and formalwear, handled with the attention and delicacy these cherished garments deserve.

Household Items

Cleaning services for curtains, drapes, tablecloths, comforters, and other household textiles that need professional-grade treatment.

Stain Treatment

Targeted stain removal for tough spots and set-in stains on garments and fabrics, using professional techniques to restore your items.
