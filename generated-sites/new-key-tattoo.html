Our Story

Art You Can Wear

New Key Tattoo is a professional tattoo studio located in downtown Gainesville, FL, dedicated to delivering exceptional custom tattoo work in a clean, welcoming environment. Whether you arrive with a fully formed vision or just a spark of an idea, our artists work closely with you to bring meaningful, lasting artwork to life.

We take pride in a careful, collaborative process — from the first sketch to the final line — so every client leaves with a piece they are proud to wear.
