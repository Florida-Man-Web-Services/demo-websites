Our Studio

Art You Can Wear

New Key Tattoo is a professional tattoo studio located in downtown Gainesville, FL, dedicated to delivering exceptional custom tattoo work in a clean, welcoming environment. Whether you arrive with a fully formed vision or just a spark of an idea, our artists work closely with you to bring meaningful, lasting artwork to life.

We take pride in maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness, safety, and artistic integrity. Every client is treated with individual attention, and every piece is approached with the care it deserves — from the initial consultation through the final line.

Located at 111 SW 3rd St in Gainesville, we serve clients from across North Central Florida. Contact us to discuss your project and schedule a consultation.
