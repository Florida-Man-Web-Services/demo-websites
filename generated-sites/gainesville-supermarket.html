About Us

A supermarket that serves the community

Gainesville Supermarket is a full-service grocery store conveniently located on W University Ave, serving families, students, and residents throughout the Gainesville area. We carry a wide range of fresh produce, pantry staples, refrigerated and frozen goods, and everyday household essentials to meet the needs of every shopper.

Our goal is to make grocery shopping easy and accessible, with friendly staff, well-stocked shelves, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you are doing your weekly big shop or a quick top-up, we are here to help.
