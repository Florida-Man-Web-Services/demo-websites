Who We Are

Rooted in Tradition.
Built for Today.

Bujinkan Zeropoint Dojo is a dedicated training center located in Gainesville, FL, offering instruction in Bujinkan Budo Taijutsu—an authentic Japanese martial arts system that encompasses ninjutsu, samurai combat, and traditional weapons training. Our curriculum draws from nine classical warrior schools passed down through generations of dedicated practitioners.

We welcome students of all backgrounds and experience levels. Whether you are stepping on the mat for the first time or continuing a lifelong martial arts journey, our dojo provides a disciplined yet welcoming environment focused on practical self-defense, personal growth, and warrior philosophy.

At Zeropoint Dojo, we believe that true martial arts training goes beyond physical technique—it cultivates awareness, resilience, and the mindset to navigate real-world challenges with clarity and calm.

What We Offer

Training Programs

Budo Taijutsu

The foundational body movement and unarmed combat system of Bujinkan—covering strikes, throws, joint locks, and evasion rooted in classical warrior traditions.

Core Curriculum

Ninjutsu

Train in the ancient art of the ninja—emphasizing strategy, adaptability, and survival skills drawn from the Togakure-ryu and related traditions of feudal Japan.

Specialty Training

Weapons Training

Study classical Japanese weapons including the bo staff, hanbo, sword, and others within the Bujinkan system, building awareness, distance management, and control.

Classical Arts

Practical Self-Defense

Scenario-based instruction designed to build real-world defensive awareness and effective responses to common threats—applicable for everyday people in everyday situations.

Applied Skills

Beginner Introductory Classes

New to martial arts? Our beginner-friendly introduction program eases you into the foundations of movement, etiquette, and technique in a supportive, no-pressure environment.

All Levels

Private Instruction

One-on-one sessions tailored to your individual goals, schedule, and pace. Accelerate your progress with personalized attention from a dedicated Bujinkan instructor.
