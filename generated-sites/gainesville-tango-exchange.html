Our Story

The Heart of Tango in Gainesville

♫
Embrace • Move • Connect

Gainesville Tango Exchange is Gainesville's dedicated home for Argentine tango, welcoming dancers of all experience levels — from absolute beginners to seasoned milongueros. We offer a warm, inclusive environment where the subtleties of tango technique, connection, and musicality are explored with passion and depth.

Our classes blend structured instruction with the social joy of the milonga floor, helping students build genuine skill while fostering a vibrant local tango community. Whether you are drawn to the elegance of the embrace or the improvisational dialogue between partners, there is a place for you here.

Located in the heart of Gainesville at 720 NW 23rd Ave, we bring world-class tango culture to North Central Florida. Contact us for details on upcoming classes, practicas, and special events.

  • All levels welcome, beginners to advanced
  • Focus on technique, musicality, and connection
  • Social dance events and practicas
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
What We Offer

Classes & Services

☺

Beginner Classes

Start your tango journey from scratch. Learn the fundamental embrace, basic walk, and partner communication in a relaxed, encouraging setting. No partner required.

♫

Intermediate & Advanced Classes

Deepen your technique with more complex vocabulary, musicality refinement, and the nuanced improvisational conversation that defines Argentine tango.

✦

Practica Sessions

Open practice sessions where dancers explore and reinforce material from class in a social, low-pressure environment with guidance available.

♩

Milonga (Social Dance)

Join our community milongas — social tango dances held in the traditional style, where the music sets the mood and the floor comes alive.

▶

Private Lessons

One-on-one or couples instruction tailored to your specific goals, schedule, and current level. Contact us for details and availability.

✨

Workshops & Special Events

Periodic intensives, guest instructor workshops, and tango-themed events that bring the broader community together. Contact us for upcoming dates.
