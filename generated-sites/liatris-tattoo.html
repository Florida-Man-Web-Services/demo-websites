About the Studio

Liatris Tattoo is a dedicated custom tattoo studio in Gainesville, FL, committed to producing thoughtful, high-quality work across a wide range of styles. Every piece is approached as a collaboration — your vision, executed with skill and care.

Whether you are coming in for your first tattoo or adding to a growing collection, the studio provides a clean, welcoming environment where artistry is always the priority.

Walk-ins are welcome when availability allows. Consultations are encouraged for larger or more complex custom projects to ensure the best possible result.
