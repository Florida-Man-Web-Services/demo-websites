Our Story

Every Line Tells A Story

Liatris Tattoo is a custom studio rooted in intention and artistry. We collaborate closely with every client to design meaningful, lasting work that reflects who you are — from delicate fine-line pieces to bold, expressive compositions.

Located in the heart of Gainesville, FL, our studio welcomes walk-ins when available. Every design is drawn with care, and every appointment is treated as a shared creative process from first sketch to final line.
