About Us

A Neighborhood Convenience Store

GATE Gas Station on NW 13th Street is a convenient fuel and shopping stop serving the Gainesville community. Whether you're commuting across town or just need a quick pick-me-up, we're here to make your stop fast and easy.

Stocked with a wide selection of beverages, snacks, and household essentials, our store is designed with your busy day in mind. We take pride in keeping things simple, accessible, and ready when you need them.
