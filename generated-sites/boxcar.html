Our Story

A Bar Built for Gainesville

CHAPTER 01

Rooted in Depot Park

Boxcar is a full-service bar nestled in the heart of Gainesville's vibrant Depot Park neighborhood, offering a laid-back atmosphere that draws locals, students, and visitors alike.

CHAPTER 02

Unpretentious & Welcoming

Whether you're stopping in for a cold craft beer after a long day or settling in for a night out with friends, Boxcar delivers the kind of unpretentious, welcoming vibe that keeps guests coming back.

CHAPTER 03

A Gathering Place

Situated at the crossroads of Gainesville's arts and outdoor community, we pride ourselves on being a gathering place where good drinks and good company are always on tap.
