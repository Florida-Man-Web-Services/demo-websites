About Us

Painting Done Right, Every Time

A Plus Painting and Repair is a locally operated painting and repair company serving Gainesville, FL and the surrounding communities. We take pride in delivering clean, professional results — from full interior refreshes to exterior coatings built to handle Florida's demanding climate.

Our team pays close attention to surface preparation, paint selection, and finishing detail, because we know that quality work starts long before a brush ever touches a wall. Whether you're updating a single room or tackling a full exterior overhaul, we approach every project with care and professionalism.

We also handle minor repair work alongside painting jobs, so you get a smooth, ready-to-paint surface without having to coordinate multiple contractors. Contact us for details on how we can help with your next project.

What We Do

Our Services

From prep work to final coat, we handle painting and repair projects of all sizes. Contact us for details on pricing and availability.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, and doors refreshed with clean lines and even coverage. Minimal disruption to your home or business.

Exterior Painting

Durable exterior coatings designed to withstand Florida's heat, humidity, and seasonal weather for long-lasting curb appeal.

Cabinet & Trim Painting

Precision painting for kitchen cabinets, baseboards, window trim, crown molding, and other detail work throughout your space.

Surface Prep & Repair

Sanding, patching drywall, filling cracks, and priming to ensure a smooth foundation before any paint is applied.

Commercial Painting

Professional painting services for offices, retail spaces, and commercial properties. Scheduled to minimize impact on your business operations.

Power Washing

Pre-paint surface cleaning and standalone power washing to remove dirt, mildew, and buildup from siding, driveways, and more.
