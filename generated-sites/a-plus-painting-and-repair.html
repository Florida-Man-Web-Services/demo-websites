About Us

Painting done right, every time.

A Plus Painting and Repair is a locally based painting and repair company serving Gainesville and the surrounding area. We handle both the brushwork and the fixes behind it — from patching drywall to laying down a flawless finish coat.

Careful surface prep, clean lines and materials chosen for Florida's climate mean your paint job looks great and holds up. We treat every property with respect and leave the site clean.

Whether it's a single room or a full exterior, contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your project.

Our Services

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim and doors with proper prep, priming and clean finish coats for a polished, lasting look.

Exterior Painting

Durable exterior coatings built to stand up to Florida heat, humidity and sun, with thorough surface prep first.
