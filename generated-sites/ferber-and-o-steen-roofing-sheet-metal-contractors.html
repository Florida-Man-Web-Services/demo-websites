About Us

Experienced Roofing & Sheet Metal Contractors

Ferber And O'Steen Roofing & Sheet Metal Contractors is a locally operated specialty contractor serving residential and commercial clients throughout Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida region. The company provides roofing installation, repair, and replacement alongside precision sheet metal fabrication and installation services.

From standing seam metal roofing to custom flashing and ductwork, the team brings hands-on expertise to every project, ensuring lasting results suited to Florida's demanding climate. Contact us to discuss your roofing or sheet metal needs.

What We Do

Our Services

Roofing Installation

New roof installation for residential and commercial properties using quality materials suited to Florida's heat and storm conditions.

Roofing Repair & Replacement

Prompt leak repairs, storm damage restoration, and full roof replacements to extend the life and performance of your roofing system.

Metal Roofing

Standing seam and metal panel roofing systems offering superior durability, energy efficiency, and long-term value for Florida properties.

Custom Sheet Metal Fabrication

Precision-cut and formed sheet metal components including gutters, downspouts, flashings, and architectural trim pieces made to specification.

Flashing & Waterproofing

Installation and replacement of roof flashings around chimneys, skylights, vents, and wall intersections to prevent moisture intrusion.

Inspections & Estimates

Professional roof and sheet metal inspections to assess current condition, identify issues early, and provide clear estimates for any needed work. Contact us for details.
