About the Dojo

Where Tradition Meets Modern Training

The Cuong Nhu Cypress Dojo at the University of Florida is a welcoming community dedicated to the practice of Cuong Nhu — a dynamic Vietnamese-American martial art that blends striking, grappling, weapons, and mindfulness into one holistic discipline.

Students of all experience levels are invited to develop physical fitness, mental resilience, and self-confidence in a structured yet supportive environment guided by certified instructors.

Whether you are brand new to martial arts or an experienced practitioner seeking to deepen your training, the Cypress Dojo provides a path for growth, respect, and lifelong learning.

Programs & Training

What We Offer

01

Beginner Classes

An introduction to the fundamentals of Cuong Nhu — stances, strikes, blocks, and basic forms — designed for those with no prior martial arts experience. Contact us for details on scheduling.

02

Forms (Kata) Training

Master the traditional kata of Cuong Nhu, developing precision, balance, and body awareness through rehearsed sequences that encode the art's core techniques.

03

Sparring & Self-Defense

Controlled sparring sessions and practical self-defense scenarios to apply learned techniques in a safe, supervised environment under instructor guidance.

04

Weapons Training

Learn traditional weapons arts including the bo staff and other implements, deepening understanding of distance, timing, and coordination. Contact us for details.

05

Rank Advancement

A structured belt progression system rewards dedication and growth. Students advance through ranks as they demonstrate mastery of techniques and the principles of Cuong Nhu.

06

Open Practice Sessions

Supplemental open-mat and practice sessions for enrolled students to refine skills, work with training partners, and reinforce lessons outside of formal class time.
