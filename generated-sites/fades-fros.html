About the Shop

Where the & means everything

Fades & Fros is a Gainesville barber shop built on craft and community. Whether you sit down for a crisp skin fade or a shaped-up fro, the goal is the same — you leave looking sharp and feeling like yourself.

We welcome every head of hair and every texture. Students, professionals, kids, and regulars all find a chair here on NW 5th Ave, where the clippers stay warm and the conversation flows easy.

A great barber shop is a neighborhood fixture. Pull up, take a seat, and let the craft do the talking.
