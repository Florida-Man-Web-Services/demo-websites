Our Story

Precision Cuts for Every Style

Fades & Fros is a Gainesville barbershop dedicated to delivering top-quality cuts for men and boys of all hair types — from tight coils and natural textures to straight and wavy hair. We take pride in a welcoming atmosphere where attention to detail and client satisfaction come first.

Rooted in the Gainesville community, our barbers bring skill and artistry to every chair, whether you're coming in for a classic fade, a shape-up, or a full style refresh. No two heads of hair are the same, and we treat every client as an individual.
