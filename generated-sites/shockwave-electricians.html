What We Do

Our Electrical Services

From routine maintenance to complex installations, we handle a full range of electrical work for Gainesville homes and businesses.

Electrical Panel Upgrades

Upgrading outdated panels to meet modern capacity demands and safety standards, including breaker replacements and service entrance work.

Interior & Exterior Lighting

Installation and replacement of indoor fixtures, recessed lighting, ceiling fans, outdoor security lights, and landscape lighting systems.

Outlet & Switch Installation

Adding new outlets, upgrading to GFCI and AFCI protection, USB outlets, and dimmer or smart switch installations throughout your property.

Residential Wiring

New-construction wiring, rewiring of older homes, dedicated circuits for appliances, and EV charger circuit installations for residential properties.

Commercial Electrical Work

Office buildouts, retail space wiring, parking lot lighting, three-phase power, and ongoing maintenance contracts for commercial clients.

Electrical Repairs & Troubleshooting

Diagnosing tripped breakers, flickering lights, faulty outlets, and other electrical issues — restoring safe and reliable power quickly.
