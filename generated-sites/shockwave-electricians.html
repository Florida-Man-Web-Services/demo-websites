What We Do

Electrical Services

Full-spectrum electrical work for Gainesville residences and businesses.

Panel Upgrades

Replacing outdated breaker panels to meet modern capacity demands and safety codes, including service entrance upgrades.

Interior & Exterior Lighting

Fixture installation and replacement, recessed lighting, ceiling fans, security lights, and landscape lighting systems.

Outlets & Switches

New outlets, GFCI and AFCI upgrades, USB outlets, dimmer switches, and smart switch installations throughout your property.

Residential Wiring

New-construction wiring, rewiring of older homes, dedicated circuits for appliances, and EV charger circuit installations.

Commercial Electrical

Office and retail buildouts, parking lot lighting, three-phase power, and ongoing maintenance for commercial properties.

Repairs & Troubleshooting

Diagnosing tripped breakers, flickering lights, dead outlets, and other electrical faults — restoring safe power fast.
