Our Studio

Ink With Intention

The Line Defined Tattoo is a professional tattoo studio in the heart of downtown Gainesville, committed to delivering custom artwork that tells your story with clarity and precision. From fine-line portraits to bold traditional pieces, every tattoo is approached with the same dedication to craft.

We believe a tattoo is more than a design — it's a permanent expression of identity. Our studio offers a clean, welcoming environment where clients feel comfortable discussing their vision and collaborating on something truly one of a kind.

We work by consultation to ensure every client receives individualized attention and an end result they'll carry with pride. Contact us to start the conversation about your next piece.
