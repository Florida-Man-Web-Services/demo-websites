About Us

Craft & Precision

Paper Cuts Barbershop is a neighborhood staple in Gainesville, dedicated to delivering precise, quality cuts in a welcoming, no-frills environment that keeps clients coming back.

From classic tapers to modern fades, the shop serves men and boys of all ages with the kind of attention to detail that makes a real difference — every single visit.

Located on SE 22nd Ave, Paper Cuts is proud to be part of the Gainesville community, offering a relaxed atmosphere where great conversation and even better cuts go hand in hand.
