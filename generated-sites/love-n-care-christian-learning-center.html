About Us

A Warm, Faith-Based Home Away From Home

Love N Care Christian Learning Center is a trusted childcare provider in Gainesville, FL dedicated to nurturing the whole child — mind, body, and spirit — through Christ-centered values and developmentally appropriate education.

Our experienced caregivers create a safe, loving environment where children of all ages can explore, discover, and build the social and academic skills they need to flourish in school and in life.

Serving the Gainesville community, we partner closely with families to ensure every child feels valued, supported, and excited to learn each day. Contact us for current enrollment availability and program details.
