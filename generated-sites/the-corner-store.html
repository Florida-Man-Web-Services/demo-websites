About Us

A Neighborhood Staple on N Main St

The Corner Store is a community convenience shop serving the residents and visitors of North Gainesville with a friendly, no-fuss shopping experience. Whether you need a quick snack, a household staple, or a cold drink, we keep the shelves stocked so you never have to go far.

Conveniently located at 1115 N Main St, we're a familiar stop for students, locals, and anyone passing through the neighborhood who needs something in a hurry. Contact us for details on any specific product availability or services.
