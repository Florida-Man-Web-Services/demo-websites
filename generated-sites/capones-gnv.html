About Us

A Gainesville
Staple

Capones GNV is a beloved bar nestled in the heart of downtown Gainesville on South Main Street — a go-to destination for locals and visitors looking for a relaxed but lively atmosphere.

Whether you're stopping in after work, catching a game, or settling in for a late night with friends, Capones delivers the kind of welcoming energy that makes every visit feel like home.

With a curated selection of drinks and a space designed for great conversation, Capones GNV stands out as one of Gainesville's most highly rated neighborhood bars. Contact us for current hours and specials.
