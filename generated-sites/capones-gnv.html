A Gainesville Staple

Where Gainesville comes to unwind — a bold neighborhood bar with the energy this city runs on.

“Where Gainesville comes to unwind. A bold neighborhood bar serving up great drinks, good vibes, and the kind of atmosphere that keeps you coming back.”

Capones GNV is a beloved bar nestled in the heart of downtown Gainesville on South Main Street — a go-to destination for locals and visitors looking for a relaxed but lively atmosphere.

Whether you’re stopping in after work, catching a game, or settling in for a late night with friends, Capones delivers the kind of welcoming energy that makes every visit feel like home.

With a curated selection of drinks and a space designed for great conversation, Capones GNV stands out as one of Gainesville’s most highly rated neighborhood bars.

The downtown location on South Main Street puts you right in the middle of everything Gainesville has to offer — easy to find, and even harder to leave.

From a bold drink selection to the kind of crowd that makes any night memorable, Capones GNV is where the city gathers.

Contact us for current hours, specials, and upcoming events. We look forward to seeing you at 104 S Main St.
