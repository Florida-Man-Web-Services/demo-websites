Real Flavor,
Real Streets

A Gainesville staple on wheels

Locos por Tacos brings the vibrant spirit of Mexican street food to the heart of Gainesville, FL, serving up generously loaded tacos, burritos, and more from a rolling kitchen that never cuts corners on flavor.

Every item is crafted with fresh ingredients, bold seasonings, and time-honored recipes that keep locals coming back again and again — whether it's a quick lunch stop or a late-night craving fix.

As a food truck rooted in the Gainesville community, Locos por Tacos prides itself on fast, friendly service and dishes that deliver a true taste of Mexico without the sit-down price tag.
