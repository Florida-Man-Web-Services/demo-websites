About Us

A Salon Rooted in the Heart of Gainesville

Sun Fresh Style is a welcoming hair salon located in Southwest Gainesville, dedicated to helping every client look and feel their absolute best. Our talented team stays current with the latest trends and techniques so you always leave the chair feeling refreshed and confident.

We take pride in creating a warm, relaxed atmosphere where both longtime locals and new faces feel right at home. Whether you are looking for a simple trim or a full transformation, we give every service the careful attention it deserves.

Conveniently situated on SW 13th St, we are proud to be a neighborhood staple for the Gainesville community. Contact us to learn more about what we can do for you.

Personalized Consultations

Quality Products

We use professional-grade products chosen to deliver beautiful, long-lasting results while caring for your hair's health.

Welcoming Atmosphere

Our salon is designed to be a comfortable, relaxing space where you can unwind from the moment you walk in.
