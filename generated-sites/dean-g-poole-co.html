About Us

A Trusted Name in Gainesville Accounting

Dean G Poole & Co is a full-service accounting firm serving individuals, families, and businesses throughout the Gainesville area. Our approach combines meticulous attention to detail with a commitment to clear, straightforward communication so clients always understand where they stand financially.

Whether you need help with annual tax preparation, small business bookkeeping, or longer-term financial planning, our team provides reliable guidance tailored to your unique situation. We take pride in building lasting relationships built on trust and accuracy.
