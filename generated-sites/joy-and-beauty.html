Our Story

A Salon Built on Passion and Care

Joy And Beauty is a full-service hair salon located in the heart of Gainesville, Florida, dedicated to helping every client look and feel their very best. Our skilled stylists bring creativity and expertise to every appointment, whether you're coming in for a fresh trim or a complete transformation.

We take pride in creating a relaxed, inclusive atmosphere where clients of all hair types and textures feel right at home. From the moment you walk through our doors, your comfort and satisfaction are our top priority.

Located conveniently at 1020 NW 23rd Ave, Suite D, we welcome walk-ins and appointments — reach out to learn what we can do for you.
