Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

Ellianos Coffee is a beloved local coffeehouse serving the Gainesville community with expertly crafted espresso beverages and specialty drinks made from premium beans. Nestled on SW 74th Dr, we are a welcoming spot for students, professionals, and neighbors alike to slow down and savor the moment.

From your morning pick-me-up to an afternoon retreat, our team is dedicated to delivering a consistently exceptional experience in every cup — because great coffee is about more than what is in the cup; it is about how it makes you feel.
