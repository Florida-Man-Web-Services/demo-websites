About Us

Notary Services That Come to You

Stelle's Mobile Notary Services provides certified notarization for individuals, businesses, and organizations throughout Gainesville and the greater Alachua County area. Rather than requiring clients to travel to an office, we come to your home, workplace, hospital, or any convenient location.

We specialize in handling time-sensitive and legally important documents with professionalism, discretion, and care. From real estate closings to healthcare directives, our goal is to make the notarization process as seamless and stress-free as possible.

Whether you need a single signature notarized or a complete loan signing handled, Stelle's is committed to reliable, accurate service that gives you peace of mind.

What We Offer

Notary Services

Real Estate & Loan Signings

Notarization for mortgage documents, deeds, refinancing packets, and closing paperwork. Contact us for details on scheduling.

Power of Attorney

Proper notarization of durable, limited, and financial power of attorney forms to ensure their legal validity.

Healthcare Directives

Notarization of living wills, advance directives, and healthcare surrogate designations for medical planning.

Affidavits & Sworn Statements

Witnessing and notarization of sworn affidavits and statements for legal, court, and administrative purposes.

Vehicle Title & DMV Documents

Notarization of vehicle titles, bill of sale forms, and other DMV-related documents for buyers and sellers.

General Notarizations

Witnessing signatures, certifying copies, and administering oaths for a wide range of personal and business documents.
