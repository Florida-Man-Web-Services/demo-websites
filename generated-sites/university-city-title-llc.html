Our Title Services

What We Offer

From title search through closing day, we provide the essential services that protect buyers, sellers, and lenders in every real estate transaction.

No. 01

Title Search & Examination

A thorough review of public records to identify any liens, encumbrances, or defects affecting your property's title history.

No. 02

Owner's Title Insurance

A one-time policy that protects your investment against covered title defects discovered after closing — for as long as you own the property.

No. 03

Lender's Title Insurance

Required by most mortgage lenders, this policy protects the lender's interest in the property for the duration of the loan.

No. 04

Real Estate Closings

Full-service settlement coordination for residential and commercial transactions, including document preparation, signing, and disbursement of funds.

No. 05

Deed Preparation

Accurate preparation and recordation of warranty deeds, quitclaim deeds, and other documents needed to transfer property ownership.

No. 06

Refinance Closings

Efficient handling of refinance transactions, including title updates and lender-required title insurance to facilitate your new loan.
