Our Studio

Coastal Soul, Lasting Craft

Gainesville Beach Tattoo Parlor is a dedicated fine-art tattoo studio serving the Gainesville community from our upstairs studio on SW 2nd Street. Our artists bring a passion for quality craftsmanship to every piece, working closely with each client to bring their vision to life with precision and care.

From your first idea to the final line, we keep the process relaxed, collaborative, and clean — so you can focus on the art you'll carry for a lifetime.
