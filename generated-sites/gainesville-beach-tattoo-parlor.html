Our Story

Permanent Art,
Lasting Impressions

Gainesville Beach Tattoo Parlor is a dedicated fine-art tattoo studio serving the Gainesville community from our upstairs studio on SW 2nd Street. Our artists bring a passion for quality craftsmanship to every piece, working closely with each client to bring their vision to life with precision and care.

Whether you arrive with a fully formed concept or just a spark of an idea, our team is committed to creating tattoos that are as unique as the people who wear them. We work across a wide range of styles, from bold traditional flash to delicate fine-line and everything in between.

Client comfort, safety, and satisfaction are our highest priorities. Every session is conducted in a clean, welcoming environment where you can relax and trust the process.

What We Do

Our Services

✎
Custom Tattoos

Fully original designs created in collaboration with you. Bring your ideas, references, or concept and we will craft a one-of-a-kind piece built around your vision.

⭐
Traditional & Neo-Traditional

Bold lines, rich color, and timeless imagery. Classic American traditional and modern neo-traditional styles with clean, lasting results.

✦
Fine Line & Blackwork

Delicate single-needle fine-line work, intricate geometric patterns, and bold blackwork for a clean, precise aesthetic.

◆
Cover-Ups & Reworks

Breathing new life into old or unwanted tattoos. Our artists can assess your existing ink and design a cover-up that truly works.

☯
Flash Tattoos

Ready-to-tattoo flash designs available in-studio. A great option if you want something beautiful without a long design consultation.

❖
Touch-Ups & Consultations

Free consultations to plan your next piece, plus touch-up appointments to keep your existing tattoos looking their best. Contact us for details.
