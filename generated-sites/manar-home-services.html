About Manar Home Services

Honest, Dependable Help for Your Home

Manar Home Services is a Gainesville-based handyperson service helping homeowners and renters keep their homes comfortable, safe, and well maintained. We focus on doing quality work, communicating clearly, and treating every home with respect.

From quick fixes to the projects you keep putting off, we handle a wide range of household repairs and improvements throughout Gainesville and the surrounding areas. Contact us for a fair estimate on your next project.
