About Us

Serving the West University Community

A familiar stop for students, commuters, and residents alike.

🏪
West Neighhour
7114 W University Ave, Gainesville FL

West Neighhour is a convenient neighborhood store located on West University Avenue, making it an easy stop for the UF-area community. Whether you are grabbing a quick snack, a cold drink, or everyday household essentials, this store is set up to make your visit fast and hassle-free.

Serving the west Gainesville corridor, West Neighhour stocks a broad selection of everyday goods so residents and commuters can get what they need without going out of their way.

We pride ourselves on a welcoming atmosphere and a well-stocked selection — the kind of neighborhood store you can count on any day of the week.
