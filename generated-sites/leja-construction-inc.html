About Us

Your Trusted Local Contractor

Leja Construction Inc is a full-service general contracting company serving Gainesville, FL and the greater North Central Florida region. We work with homeowners, property managers, and commercial clients to deliver quality construction from the ground up.

Whether you are planning a new build, a major renovation, or a structural repair, our team brings hands-on expertise and a commitment to doing the job right the first time. We coordinate every phase of your project so you have a single, reliable point of contact throughout.

We take pride in honest communication, clean job sites, and work that stands up over time. Contact us to discuss your project and what we can build together.
