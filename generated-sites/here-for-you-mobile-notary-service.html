What We Offer

Mobile Notary Services

From routine personal documents to complex loan closings, we handle a wide range of notarization needs throughout the Gainesville area.

General Document Notarization

Affidavits, powers of attorney, wills, contracts, and other personal or business documents requiring a licensed notary signature and seal.

Loan Signing Services

Certified signing agent services for real estate closings, refinances, home equity loans, and mortgage document packages.

Power of Attorney

Notarization of durable, medical, financial, and limited power of attorney documents to ensure they are legally recognized.

Hospital & Care Facility Visits

Compassionate in-person visits to hospitals, nursing homes, and assisted living facilities for patients who cannot travel.
