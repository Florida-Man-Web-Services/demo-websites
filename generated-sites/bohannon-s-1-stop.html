About Us

Trusted Tire Service in Gainesville

Bohannon's 1 Stop is a neighborhood tire shop located on North Main Street in Gainesville, FL, serving drivers across Alachua County with honest, reliable service. Whether you need a new set of tires, a quick rotation, or an alignment check before a long trip, the team here handles it with a no-nonsense approach and a focus on getting you back on the road safely.

As a local business, Bohannon's takes pride in straightforward pricing and the kind of personal attention you won't find at a big chain. From everyday passenger vehicles to trucks, they're equipped to handle what Gainesville drivers need most.

Stop by the shop on North Main Street or give them a call — contact us for details on availability and current wait times.
