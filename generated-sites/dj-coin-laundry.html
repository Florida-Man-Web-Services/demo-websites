Self-Service Laundry

DJ Coin Laundry is a self-service coin laundromat conveniently located on SE Hawthorne Rd in Gainesville, Florida, serving the local community with accessible washing and drying facilities.

Whether you are a student, a busy professional, or anyone in between, our machines are available to handle loads of all sizes — from everyday garments to bulky items that won't fit in a home machine.

We aim to keep our facility available and operational so Gainesville residents can take care of their laundry needs quickly and efficiently.
