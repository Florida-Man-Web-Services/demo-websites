The Front Page • On Craft

Where the chair is a barbershop tradition, kept sharp.

Kutters Barber Shop is a neighborhood barbershop in Gainesville, Florida, dedicated to the timeless craft of a clean cut and a close shave. Step through the door and the ritual is the same as it has always been — the snap of the cape, the buzz of the clippers, and a finish that leaves you looking sharp.

Every service is treated as a piece of handiwork. From tight skin fades and precise tapers to full beard shaping and the classic hot-towel straight-razor shave, the work is done with patience and pride.

Located on NE 16th Ave, the shop serves students, professionals, families, and everyone in between. No fuss, no gimmicks — just honest barbering and a chair that keeps the neighborhood looking its best.

“A great cut isn't rushed — it's built, line by clean line.” The Kutters Standard
