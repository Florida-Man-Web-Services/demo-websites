What We Do

Electrical Services for Every Need

From single-outlet repairs to full panel replacements, we offer a comprehensive range of residential and commercial electrical solutions across Gainesville and the surrounding area.

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

Outdated or undersized electrical panels can be a safety hazard. We assess, upgrade, and replace breaker panels to meet modern load demands and code requirements.

Electrical Troubleshooting & Repair

Tripped breakers, flickering lights, dead outlets — we diagnose the root cause and get your electrical system back to working order safely and efficiently.

Residential Wiring

New construction, additions, or rewiring older homes — we install safe, code-compliant wiring tailored to your home's specific layout and power requirements.

Outlet & Switch Installation

Adding new outlets, upgrading to GFCI/AFCI protection, or replacing worn switches — we handle all outlet and switch work quickly and to code.

Lighting Installation

Interior and exterior lighting upgrades, ceiling fan wiring, recessed lighting, and security lighting — we make sure every fixture is safely installed and properly illuminated.

Commercial Electrical Services

We support local Gainesville businesses with commercial electrical needs including tenant build-outs, service upgrades, and ongoing maintenance. Contact us for details.
