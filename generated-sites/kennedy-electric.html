What We Do

Electrical Services for Every Need

From a single outlet to a full commercial build-out, Kennedy Electric delivers safe, code-compliant electrical work across Gainesville and North Central Florida.

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

Outdated panels are a safety hazard. We assess, upgrade, and replace breaker panels to handle modern loads safely and to current code.

Troubleshooting & Repair

Tripped breakers, dead outlets, flickering lights — we diagnose and fix electrical problems so your home or business stays safe and powered.

Residential Wiring

New construction, additions, or rewiring older homes — we install safe, code-compliant wiring tailored to your home's layout and power demands.

Outlet & Switch Installation

Adding new outlets, upgrading to GFCI/AFCI protection, or replacing worn switches — all work completed quickly and to code.

Lighting Installation

Interior and exterior lighting upgrades, recessed lighting, ceiling fan wiring, and security lights — installed safely and professionally.

Commercial Electrical
