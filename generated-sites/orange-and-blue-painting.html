About Us

Gainesville's trusted painting professionals

Orange and Blue Painting is a locally owned painting company proudly serving Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida community. We specialize in both interior and exterior work, bringing care and precision to every project — from single rooms to full commercial builds.

Our team is committed to quality prep work, clean lines and materials that stand up to Florida's climate, so your paint job looks great and lasts. We treat every property with respect and leave the job site clean when we're done.

Whether you're refreshing a room, updating curb appeal or tackling a larger renovation, we're here to help. Contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your project.

What We Do

Our Painting Services

Interior Painting

Full interior painting for living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens and more. We handle prep, priming and clean finish coats for a polished look that lasts.

Exterior Painting

Durable exterior coatings applied to withstand Florida heat, humidity and sun. Includes thorough surface prep, caulking and weather-resistant paint systems.

Commercial Painting

Office buildings, retail spaces and multi-unit properties. We work efficiently to minimize disruption and deliver professional results on schedule.

Cabinet & Trim Painting

Specialty coatings for cabinets, baseboards, doors and trim — precision work that refreshes the detail areas that make a big impact in any room.

Drywall Repair & Prep

Surface repair, patching and skim coating before paint is applied. Proper prep is the foundation of any long-lasting paint job.

Color Consultation

Not sure which color is right? We can help guide your selections to find tones that complement your space, lighting and style goals. Contact us for details.
