About Us

A Gainesville Neighborhood Staple

Kelly's Kwik Stop is a convenient, no-fuss stop on SW 34th Street in Gainesville, serving the everyday needs of students, commuters, and residents in the surrounding community. Whether you need a quick snack, a cold drink, or a few household staples, we keep the shelves stocked so you're never left empty-handed.
