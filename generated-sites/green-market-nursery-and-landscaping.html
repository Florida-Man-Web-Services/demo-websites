About Us

Your Local Plant and Landscape Experts

Green Market Nursery and Landscaping is a full-service plant nursery and landscaping company serving the Gainesville community and surrounding areas of Alachua County. We carry a wide selection of Florida-friendly plants, trees, shrubs, and garden supplies suited to our region's unique climate.

Our knowledgeable team is passionate about helping homeowners and businesses create beautiful, thriving outdoor spaces. Whether you are looking for the perfect specimen plant, native Florida species, or a complete landscape transformation, we are here to help.

From our convenient location on NW 8th Ave, we are proud to be a trusted resource for gardeners and landscaping professionals throughout North Central Florida. Contact us for details on availability and services.
