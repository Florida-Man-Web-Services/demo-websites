Who We Are

Exceptional Custom Artwork

G Tattoos is a professional tattoo and piercing studio dedicated to delivering exceptional custom artwork in a clean, welcoming environment. Our skilled artists work closely with each client to bring their ideas to life with precision and care.

Whether you are looking for bold traditional designs, fine-line detail work, or a safe and sterile piercing experience, our studio upholds the highest standards of hygiene and artistic quality at every step. We are proud to serve the Gainesville community and welcome clients of all backgrounds and experience levels — from first-timers to seasoned collectors.
