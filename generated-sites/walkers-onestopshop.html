About Us

Gainesville's Go-To Custom Apparel Shop

Walkers OneStopShop is a custom t-shirt and apparel destination serving Gainesville and the surrounding North Florida community. Whether you need gear for a local business, sports team, school event, or personal project, we bring your vision to life with quality printing and a wide range of apparel options.

We take pride in delivering fast turnarounds and attentive service, making the custom printing process as simple and stress-free as possible. From single pieces to large bulk orders, we work with customers to find the right fit for every budget and occasion.

Stop by our shop on SE 1st Ave or give us a call — we are happy to walk you through your options and help you create something you will love wearing.
