What we do

Tire Sales & Installation

A broad selection for cars, trucks, and SUVs, mounted and installed to manufacturer specs. Contact us for current inventory and pricing.

Flat Tire Repair

Punctured or leaking? We assess whether a safe repair is possible, then patch or plug it properly to get you moving again.

Wheel Balancing

Unbalanced wheels cause vibration and uneven wear. We balance for a smooth ride and longer tire life.

Wheel Alignment

We check and adjust camber, caster, and toe to factory specs so your vehicle tracks straight and tires wear evenly.

Tire Rotation

Regular rotation promotes even tread wear across all four tires and keeps handling consistent — great to pair with an oil change.

Pressure & Inspection

Correct pressure is critical for safety and fuel economy. We inspect and check pressure to catch issues early.
