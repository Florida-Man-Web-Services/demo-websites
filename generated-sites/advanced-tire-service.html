What We Do

Our Services

Tire Sales & Installation

We offer a broad selection of tires for passenger cars, trucks, and SUVs. Our team handles mounting and installation to manufacturer specifications. Contact us for details on current inventory and pricing.

Flat Tire Repair

Punctured or leaking tire? We assess whether a safe repair is possible and patch or plug it properly, restoring your tire's integrity and getting you moving again quickly.

Wheel Balancing

Unbalanced wheels cause vibration, uneven tread wear, and premature tire wear. We balance your wheels to ensure a smooth, comfortable ride and extend tire life.

Wheel Alignment

Proper alignment protects your tires from irregular wear and keeps your vehicle tracking straight. We check and adjust camber, caster, and toe angles to factory specifications.

Tire Rotation

Regular tire rotation promotes even tread wear across all four tires, maximizing their lifespan and maintaining consistent handling. We recommend rotation at each oil change interval.

Tire Pressure & Inspection

Correct tire pressure is critical for safety, fuel efficiency, and tire longevity. We perform thorough tire inspections and pressure checks to catch issues before they become problems.
