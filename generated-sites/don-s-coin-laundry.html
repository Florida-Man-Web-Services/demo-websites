About Us

A Community Laundromat You Can Count On

Don's Coin Laundry is a dependable self-service laundromat serving the Gainesville community from its convenient location at 5001 NW 34th Blvd. We offer a clean, accessible space with multiple washer and dryer options to handle loads of all sizes.

Whether you're a student, a busy family, or just need a reliable spot to get your laundry done, our facility is designed to make the process as quick and hassle-free as possible.

We take pride in maintaining well-functioning equipment and a tidy environment so every visit is a comfortable one. Contact us for details on current availability and pricing.
