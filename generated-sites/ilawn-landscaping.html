About Us

Dedicated to Beautiful Lawns in Gainesville

iLawn & Landscaping provides dependable, high-quality lawn care and landscaping services to homeowners and businesses throughout the Gainesville area. We understand the unique challenges of Florida's climate and tailor our approach to help your property thrive in the heat and humidity of North Central Florida.

From routine mowing and edging to full landscape design and installation, our team handles every job with care and attention to detail. We take pride in punctual, professional service that keeps your outdoor spaces healthy and looking their best season after season.

Ready to transform your lawn? Contact us for a consultation — we'll walk through your property and put together a plan that fits your needs and budget.
