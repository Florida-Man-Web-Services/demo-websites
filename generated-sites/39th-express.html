About Us

Convenient & Reliable

39th Express is a neighborhood gas station and convenience store serving drivers throughout the Gainesville area. Located along one of the city's busiest corridors, we aim to make every stop quick, easy, and hassle-free.

From filling up your tank to picking up everyday essentials, we keep the things you need in stock so you can spend less time stopping and more time moving. Our central location on NW 39th Ave makes us a natural choice for commuters, students, and residents alike.
