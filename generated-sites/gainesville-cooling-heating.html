About Us

Your Local HVAC Specialists

Gainesville Cooling & Heating provides reliable heating, ventilation, and air conditioning services to homeowners and businesses throughout the Gainesville area. Florida's heat and humidity demand HVAC systems you can count on, and our team is equipped to keep yours running at its best.

We handle everything from routine maintenance and system tune-ups to full equipment installations and emergency repairs. Whether your AC has stopped cooling on a hot summer day or your heat is struggling in the cooler months, we respond quickly and work to resolve the problem right the first time.

Our goal is straightforward: honest assessments, quality workmanship, and comfort restored as fast as possible. Contact us for details on pricing or service availability.
