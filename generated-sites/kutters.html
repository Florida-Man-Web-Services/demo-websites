About the Shop

A Cut Built on Craft

Kutters is a neighborhood barber shop in Gainesville, Florida, where a good cut is treated like a craft. Walk in, take a seat, and leave looking sharp.

Located on NE Waldo Rd, we serve students, professionals, and families across the area with the kind of steady, honest barbering that keeps folks coming back.

More than a haircut — it's the chair, the clippers, and the conversation. That's the Kutters way.
