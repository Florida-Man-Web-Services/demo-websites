About Us

A Cut Above the Rest

Kutters is a neighborhood barber shop rooted in the Gainesville community, delivering precision cuts and classic grooming services in a relaxed, no-frills environment that keeps clients looking their best.

Whether you're a longtime regular or stepping into the chair for the first time, our barbers take pride in understanding exactly what you want and executing it with care — every single visit.

Conveniently located on NE Waldo Rd, Kutters is the go-to spot for Gainesville locals who value a great cut without the wait or the fuss.
