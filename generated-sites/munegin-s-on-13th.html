About

A Local Bar with Character

Munegin's On 13th is a laid-back neighborhood bar situated on NW 13th Street in the heart of Gainesville. Whether you're unwinding after work, watching the game with friends, or just looking for a familiar spot with a cold drink in hand, this is the kind of place that feels like home.

Serving the Gainesville community, Munegin's offers a relaxed atmosphere where regulars and newcomers alike are welcome. The vibe is easy, the drinks are honest, and the crowd is always good.

Located conveniently at 4005 NW 13th St, Munegin's On 13th is easy to find and even easier to enjoy. Stop by and see what keeps our guests coming back.
