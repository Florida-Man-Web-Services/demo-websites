About Us

A Clean Car Is Just Around the Corner

Oasis Car Wash is a locally operated car wash conveniently located on NW 39th Ave in Gainesville, Florida, serving drivers from the surrounding neighborhoods and beyond. We're committed to thorough, reliable results so you can get back on the road looking your best.

Whether you need a quick exterior rinse or a more detailed clean, we offer options to fit your schedule. Our setup is built for a smooth, efficient visit with minimal wait time.

What We Offer

Our Services

From a basic rinse to a full-service treatment, we have options to keep your vehicle in top shape. Contact us for details on pricing and availability.

Exterior Wash

A thorough rinse and wash of the full exterior to remove dirt, dust, and road grime.

Interior Vacuuming

Vacuum service for floors, seats, and cargo areas to keep the inside fresh and tidy.

Foam Pre-Soak

High-foam pre-soak that loosens heavy dirt and road contaminants before the main wash.

Wheel & Tire Cleaning

Targeted cleaning of wheels and tires to remove brake dust and road buildup.

Spot-Free Rinse

A final rinse using treated water to minimize water spots and keep the paint bright.

Full-Service Wash

A comprehensive package combining exterior cleaning, interior vacuuming, and window wipe-down.
