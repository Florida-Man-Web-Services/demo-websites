About Us

A Clean Car Is Just Around the Corner

Oasis Car Wash is a locally operated car wash conveniently located on NW 39th Ave in Gainesville, Florida, serving drivers from the surrounding neighborhoods and beyond. We are committed to delivering thorough, reliable results so you can get back on the road looking your best.

Whether you need a quick exterior rinse or a more detailed clean, we offer options to fit your schedule and needs. Our setup is designed for a smooth, efficient visit with minimal wait time.

Stop by and experience the difference that attentive, quality car care can make — contact us for current availability and service details.

What We Offer

Our Services

From a basic rinse to a full detail treatment, we have options to keep your vehicle in top shape. Contact us for details on pricing and availability.

Exterior Wash

A thorough rinse and wash of the full exterior to remove dirt, dust, and road grime. Contact us for details.

Interior Vacuuming

Vacuum service for floors, seats, and cargo areas to keep the inside of your car fresh and tidy. Contact us for details.

Foam Pre-Soak

High-foam pre-soak treatment that loosens heavy dirt and road contaminants before the main wash cycle. Contact us for details.

Wheel & Tire Cleaning

Targeted cleaning of wheels and tires to remove brake dust and road buildup for a complete, polished finish. Contact us for details.

Spot-Free Rinse

A final spot-free rinse using treated water to minimize water spots and leave your paint looking brilliant. Contact us for details.

Full-Service Wash

A comprehensive wash package combining exterior cleaning, interior vacuuming, and window wipe-down. Contact us for details.
