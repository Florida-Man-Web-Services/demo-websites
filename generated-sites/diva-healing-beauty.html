Our Story

A Sanctuary for the Body & Spirit

Diva + Healing Beauty is a boutique day spa nestled in Gainesville, FL, dedicated to providing a tranquil retreat from everyday stress. Our skilled therapists blend time-honored healing techniques with modern wellness practices to create a deeply restorative experience for every guest.

Whether you are seeking tension relief, radiant skin, or a moment of pure relaxation, our cozy suite on SW 75th Street offers a warm and welcoming environment tailored to your well-being. We believe that true beauty begins with inner healing.

Every visit is customized to your individual needs — contact us for details on available sessions and scheduling.
