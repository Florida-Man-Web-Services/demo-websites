About Us

Gainesville's Custom T-Shirt Shop

T-Desk Designs is a custom apparel shop serving the Gainesville, FL community with personalized t-shirts and printed garments for every occasion — from school events and sports teams to local businesses and personal projects.

We work closely with every customer to ensure designs are printed with care, accurate colors, and lasting quality. Whether you need a single shirt or a bulk order, we're here to make the process easy and the results impressive.

Stop by our location on NW 4th St or give us a call to discuss your vision — we're happy to help you create something you'll be proud to wear.
