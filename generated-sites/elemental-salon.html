Our Story

A Salon Built on Connection

Elemental Salon is a boutique hair salon nestled in Gainesville, FL, dedicated to delivering personalized, high-quality hair services in a relaxed and welcoming environment.

Our stylists take pride in listening to each client's vision and bringing it to life with skill and intention — whether you're after a clean cut, a bold color transformation, or a nourishing treatment.

We believe great hair begins with great relationships. From your first visit, you'll feel the difference that genuine care and attention to detail makes.
