About Us

Crafting Light & Shadow
Across Gainesville

Gourmet Landscaping Services Inc is a dedicated landscape lighting design firm serving homeowners and businesses throughout the Gainesville, FL area. We specialize in creating custom outdoor lighting environments that enhance curb appeal, improve safety, and extend the enjoyment of your property well after sundown.

Our design approach blends artistry with technical precision — carefully placing fixtures to highlight architectural features, mature trees, garden beds, and water elements. Whether you are seeking a subtle ambient glow or a dramatic statement, we tailor every installation to your property's unique character.

From the initial design consultation through professional installation and ongoing maintenance, our team is committed to delivering results that exceed expectations. Contact us for details on how we can transform your outdoor spaces.

What We Do

Landscape Lighting Services

From concept to completion, we offer a full range of outdoor lighting solutions designed to beautify, secure, and extend your outdoor living experience.

Outdoor Lighting Design

Custom-crafted lighting plans that work with your landscape's natural contours, plant placement, and architectural features to create a cohesive, stunning nighttime aesthetic.

Architectural & Facade Lighting

Highlight your home or building's distinctive features — columns, rooflines, stonework, and entryways — with carefully angled up-lights and wash fixtures.

Tree & Garden Spotlighting

Dramatic up-lighting of specimen trees, palms, and ornamental plantings to create depth, shadow play, and visual interest throughout your garden.

Pathway & Step Lighting

Safe, elegantly lit walkways, driveways, and steps using low-profile bollards and recessed fixtures that guide guests while enhancing the overall design.

Water Feature & Pool Lighting

Submersible and perimeter lighting for fountains, ponds, and swimming pools that creates a magical, reflective ambiance in your outdoor entertaining area.

System Maintenance & Upgrades

Ongoing service, seasonal adjustments, bulb and fixture replacements, and LED retrofit upgrades to keep your lighting system performing beautifully year-round.
