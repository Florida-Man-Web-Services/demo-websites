Our Cleaning Services

Standard House Cleaning

A comprehensive top-to-bottom clean of your home, covering all main living areas, surfaces, floors, and fixtures. Contact us for details.

Deep Cleaning

An intensive cleaning session targeting hard-to-reach areas, built-up grime, baseboards, appliance interiors, and more. Ideal for seasonal refreshes.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Get your home spotless before moving in or handing the keys back. We make sure every room is fresh and ready for the next chapter.

Kitchen & Bathroom Cleaning

Focused deep-clean of your kitchen and bathrooms — the two areas that matter most for hygiene — including tiles, grout, fixtures, and appliances.

Recurring Cleaning Plans

Keep your home consistently clean with scheduled weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits. Contact us to set up a plan that fits your lifestyle.

Post-Event Cleaning

Had a gathering or celebration at home? Let us handle the cleanup so you can relax after the festivities. Contact us for details on scheduling.
