About Us

A Cut Above the Rest

Classic Cutz is a neighborhood barber shop serving the Gainesville community with precision cuts, clean fades, and attentive grooming services in a welcoming, no-fuss atmosphere.

Whether you're after a sharp lineup, a fresh shape-up, or a relaxing hot-towel shave, the barbers here take pride in delivering consistent, quality results that keep clients coming back.

Conveniently located on NW 6th St, Classic Cutz is the go-to spot for men and young adults looking for professional grooming right in the heart of Gainesville.
