About Us

Locally Rooted in Gainesville

GV Lawn Care Services is a locally owned and operated lawn care company proudly serving homeowners and businesses throughout the Gainesville, Florida area. We understand the unique demands of North Florida's climate and keep your outdoor spaces looking their best year-round.

From routine mowing and edging to more intensive yard cleanups and landscaping maintenance, our team brings attention to detail and dependable scheduling to every property we care for.
