About the Shop

Precision in the heart of Gainesville

Cutz by Dray brings a focused, no-nonsense approach to barbering in Gainesville. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a regular client, you can count on consistent quality and attention to detail that keeps you looking sharp.

Located in the NW 6th Street corridor, the shop serves the broader Gainesville community with a welcoming atmosphere and a commitment to craft. From classic taper fades to modern line-up work, the focus is always on the cut.
