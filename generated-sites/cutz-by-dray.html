About the Shop

Precision Cuts in the Heart of Gainesville

Cutz by Dray brings a focused, no-nonsense approach to barbering in Gainesville. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a regular client, you can count on consistent quality and attention to detail that keeps you looking sharp.

Located in the NW 6th Street corridor, the shop serves the broader Gainesville community with a welcoming atmosphere and a commitment to craft. Every visit is treated as an opportunity to deliver a clean, confident look tailored to you.

From classic taper fades to modern line-up work, the focus is always on the cut — done right, every time. For details on availability or specific services, contact us directly.
