About Us

Your neighborhood discount store

Roses Express is a go-to discount destination on North Main Street, serving the Gainesville community with a wide assortment of everyday products at prices that fit every budget.

Whether you are stocking up on household essentials, personal care items, seasonal goods, or grab-and-go snacks, our store offers a convenient one-stop shopping experience without the big-box price tag. We are proud to be a part of the North Gainesville neighborhood and to make value-priced shopping accessible and welcoming for families and individuals alike.
