About Us

Lawn Care You Can Count On

Gainesville Lawn Care provides dependable, professional lawn maintenance services throughout the Gainesville area, helping homeowners and property managers keep their outdoor spaces in excellent condition year-round.

Florida's warm climate and frequent rainfall call for attentive, knowledgeable lawn care — and our team understands the local landscape, soil types, and turf grasses that thrive here.

From routine mowing to seasonal treatments, we deliver reliable service tailored to each property's needs. Contact us for details on scheduling and service availability.

What We Do

Our Services

Lawn Mowing & Trimming

Regular mowing, edging, and trimming to keep your lawn neat and evenly cut throughout the growing season.

Fertilization & Weed Control

Customized fertilization programs and targeted weed treatments to promote thick, healthy turf and suppress unwanted growth.

Irrigation & Watering

Irrigation system checks and watering management to ensure your lawn receives the right amount of moisture in Florida's variable climate.

Shrub & Hedge Trimming

Professional shaping and maintenance of hedges, shrubs, and ornamental plants to enhance curb appeal and healthy growth.

Leaf & Debris Removal

Thorough cleanup of leaves, clippings, and lawn debris to keep your property looking tidy and well-maintained.

Lawn Health Assessments

On-site evaluations to identify turf issues such as pest activity, disease, or drainage problems and recommend appropriate treatments.
