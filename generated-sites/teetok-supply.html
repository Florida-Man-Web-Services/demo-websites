About Us

More Than Just Clothing

TeeTok Supply is a clothing boutique located in the heart of Gainesville on W University Ave, serving students, locals, and anyone who wants to look good without the hassle. We carry a curated mix of casual and streetwear-inspired apparel suited for Florida's relaxed lifestyle.

Whether you're shopping for everyday staples or hunting for something with a bit more personality, our selection is refreshed regularly to keep things interesting. We believe good style should be accessible, comfortable, and uniquely yours.

Stop by the store or reach out to learn more about what we currently have in stock. We're happy to help you find the right fit.
