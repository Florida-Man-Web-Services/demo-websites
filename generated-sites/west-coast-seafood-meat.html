About Us

Your local source for quality seafood & meat

West Coast Seafood Meat has been serving the Gainesville community as a trusted neighborhood market for seafood lovers and meat enthusiasts alike. Our selection includes a wide variety of fresh fish, shellfish, and premium cuts sourced to bring real quality to your kitchen.

Whether you are cooking for a weeknight dinner or a weekend gathering, we stock the variety and quantity to meet your needs. Our staff is knowledgeable and happy to help you choose the right product for any occasion. Conveniently located on NE Waldo Road, we serve households, home cooks, and food service customers throughout the Gainesville area. Contact us for details on availability and current selections.
