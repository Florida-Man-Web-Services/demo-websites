About Us

Your Gainesville Roofing & Insulation Experts

BECT Attic Insulation & Roofing Services Inc serves homeowners and businesses across Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida area, providing dependable roofing and attic insulation solutions tailored to our region's demanding climate.

From storm-related repairs to full roof replacements and energy-saving insulation upgrades, our team brings the hands-on expertise needed to keep Florida homes protected, comfortable, and efficient year-round.

What We Do

Our Services

Roof Replacement

Full removal and installation of new roofing systems — shingle, metal, or specialty materials — matched to your home's needs and Florida building codes.

Roof Repair

Targeted repair of damaged, missing, or leaking sections — including post-storm assessments to restore your roof's integrity quickly and correctly.

Attic Insulation Installation

Professional installation of blown-in or batt insulation to improve energy efficiency, reduce utility bills, and keep your Gainesville home cooler in summer.

Insulation Removal & Replacement

Safe removal of old, damaged, or contaminated attic insulation followed by fresh installation to restore thermal performance and indoor air quality.

Roof Inspection

Thorough visual and structural inspections to identify wear, potential leaks, ventilation issues, and storm damage — ideal before buying or selling a home.

Ventilation & Air Sealing

Attic air sealing and ventilation improvements that work alongside insulation to control moisture, reduce heat gain, and extend the life of your roof system.
