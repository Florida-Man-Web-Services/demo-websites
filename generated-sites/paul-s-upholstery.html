About Us

Quality Craftsmanship You Can Count On

Paul's Upholstery is a trusted auto upholstery shop serving Gainesville, FL and the surrounding area. We specialize in restoring, repairing, and customizing vehicle interiors with quality materials and careful attention to detail.

Whether your seats need repair after everyday wear or you want a full custom interior upgrade, our shop is equipped to handle jobs of all sizes. We work on cars, trucks, boats, and more — bringing interiors back to life.

What We Do

Auto Upholstery Services

From minor repairs to full custom interiors, we offer a complete range of upholstery services. Contact us for a quote on your specific project.

🚗

Seat Repair & Restoration

Torn, worn, or faded seats restored to their original look and feel using premium materials matched to your vehicle's interior.

🌟

Custom Seat Covers

Professionally fitted custom covers made from leather, vinyl, cloth, or specialty fabrics — tailored to your style and specifications.

🔌

Headliner Replacement

Sagging or stained headliners replaced cleanly and professionally, restoring the look of your vehicle's interior ceiling.

🔧

Door Panel Upholstery

Door panel fabric or vinyl replacement to match or complement your existing interior, giving your vehicle a refreshed finish.

Boat & Marine Upholstery

UV-resistant and waterproof marine-grade upholstery for boat seats, benches, and cushions built to withstand the Florida sun.

Complete interior overhauls for classic cars, trucks, and specialty vehicles. Contact us for details and to discuss your vision.
