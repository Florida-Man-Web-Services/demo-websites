About Us

Quality Craftsmanship You Can Count On

Paul's Upholstery is a trusted auto upholstery shop serving Gainesville, FL and the surrounding area. We specialize in restoring, repairing, and customizing vehicle interiors with quality materials and careful attention to detail.

Whether your seats need repair after everyday wear or you want a full custom interior upgrade, our shop is equipped to handle jobs of all sizes. We work on cars, trucks, boats, and more — bringing interiors back to life.
