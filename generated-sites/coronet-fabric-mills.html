Our Story

A Trusted Name in Fabric & Textiles

Coronet Fabric Mills has been a cornerstone destination for sewists, crafters, and design professionals across the Gainesville area. Our store carries a wide-ranging inventory of fabrics — from everyday quilting cottons to specialty apparel and upholstery textiles — so you can find exactly what your project calls for.

Whether you are a seasoned seamstress, a home decorator, or just picking up a needle and thread for the first time, our knowledgeable team is here to help you navigate our extensive selection and find the materials that bring your vision to life.

We are proud to be the local resource that keeps creative projects moving forward — one yard at a time.
