About Us

Local Lawn Care, Done Right

Theo's Lawn Maintenance is a locally owned and operated lawn care company serving the Gainesville, FL community. We take pride in delivering reliable, high-quality service tailored to the unique landscape needs of North Central Florida's climate.

From routine mowing to seasonal clean-ups, our team approaches every property with attention to detail and a commitment to consistent results. Whether you're a homeowner or a business, we keep your outdoor spaces healthy, tidy, and inviting year-round.

We build long-term relationships with our clients by showing up on time, communicating clearly, and doing the job right the first time. Contact us today to learn how we can help with your lawn.
