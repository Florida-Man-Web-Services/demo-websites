About Us

Trusted Detailing in Gainesville

Sowell Wash & Wax is a dedicated car detailing service serving Gainesville and the surrounding North Florida area. We take pride in delivering thorough, attentive care to every vehicle that comes through our doors — from a quick exterior wash to a full interior restoration.

Our team uses quality products and proven techniques to protect your vehicle's finish and keep your interior fresh. Whether you're prepping for a special occasion or just want your daily driver looking its best, we've got you covered.

Conveniently located at 1954 N Main St, we make it easy for Gainesville residents and visitors to keep their vehicles in top shape. Contact us today to learn about our current offerings and availability.
