About Us

North Central Florida's Trusted HVAC Experts

AAA Refrigeration is a Gainesville-based HVAC contractor serving homeowners and businesses across north central Florida. We specialize in heating, cooling, and refrigeration systems — providing honest, dependable service in a climate where comfort is non-negotiable.

Our technicians are experienced with all major makes and models, from residential air conditioners to commercial refrigeration units. Whether you need a quick repair, a full system installation, or routine maintenance, we approach every job with professionalism and care.

We're proud to have earned the trust of our community through consistent quality and straightforward communication. Contact us for details on how we can help with your specific needs.

What We Do

Our Services

From installation to emergency repairs, we handle all aspects of HVAC and refrigeration for residential and commercial customers.

AC Installation & Replacement

Professional installation of new central air conditioning systems, ductless mini-splits, and heat pumps sized right for your space.

AC Repair & Diagnostics

Fast, accurate troubleshooting and repair of malfunctioning air conditioners — keeping your home or business comfortable when it matters most.

Residential HVAC Services

Complete heating and cooling solutions for homes — including system tune-ups, filter changes, and ductwork inspections.

Commercial Refrigeration

Installation, maintenance, and repair of commercial refrigerators, freezers, and walk-in cooler systems for restaurants and businesses.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled inspections and tune-ups that extend equipment life, improve efficiency, and help prevent costly breakdowns before they happen.

Refrigerant Services

Certified refrigerant recovery, recharge, and leak detection services performed in compliance with EPA regulations.
