Our Story

A cozy corner of Gainesville worth savoring.

Nestled along West University Avenue, Daydreamer+ Cafe is a warm retreat for students, locals, and visitors who crave quality coffee and a welcoming atmosphere. From carefully crafted espresso drinks to freshly prepared bites, everything here is made with intention.

Whether you are settling in with a laptop for a few hours or catching up with friends over a slow pour, Daydreamer+ offers the kind of unhurried, comfortable space that Gainesville coffee lovers keep coming back to. Come in, get comfortable, and let your mind wander.
