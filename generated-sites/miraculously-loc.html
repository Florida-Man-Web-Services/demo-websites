Our Story

Rooted in the art of locs

Miraculously Loc is a dedicated locs and natural hair salon serving the Gainesville community with specialized expertise in locking techniques, maintenance, and styling for all hair types and textures.

Nestled in the heart of Gainesville on NW 13th Street, our salon provides a welcoming, affirming space where clients can embrace their natural beauty and receive personalized attention from start to finish.

Whether you are beginning your loc journey or maintaining a mature set, we tailor every appointment to your unique hair goals. Contact us for details on scheduling and what to expect at your visit.

What We Offer

Our Services

Loc Installation

Start your loc journey with a professional installation using your preferred method — freeform, interlocking, or two-strand twists. Contact us for details.

Loc Maintenance & Retwist

Keep your locs neat, healthy, and thriving with routine retwist and maintenance appointments tailored to your hair's growth cycle.

Loc Styling

From updos and pinned styles to loc extensions and decorative accessories, we create looks that reflect your personal expression.

Scalp Treatments

Nourish and refresh your scalp with targeted treatments designed to promote healthy hair growth and relieve dryness or buildup.

Loc Repair & Fusing

Restore locs that have experienced thinning, breakage, or unraveling with our professional repair and fusing techniques.

Natural Hair Consultations

Not sure where to start? Book a one-on-one consultation to discuss your hair goals, texture, and the best path forward for your loc journey.
