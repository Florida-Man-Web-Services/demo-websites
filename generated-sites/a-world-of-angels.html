About Us

A Home Away From Home

A World of Angels is a warm and welcoming day care center serving families throughout Gainesville, FL, offering attentive, individualized care for children in a safe and stimulating environment. Our caregivers are dedicated to fostering each child's social, emotional, and developmental growth through age-appropriate activities and play-based learning. We take pride in being a trusted partner for local families, giving parents peace of mind while their children thrive in our care.

What We Offer

Programs & Services

We offer a range of care programs designed to support children's growth and give parents flexibility. Contact us for details on enrollment and scheduling.

👶
Infant & Toddler Care

Gentle, attentive care tailored to the unique developmental needs of infants and toddlers, with nurturing one-on-one interaction throughout the day.

🤓
Preschool Program

Structured yet playful learning activities that prepare young children for kindergarten, building early literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

🌞
Full-Day Child Care

Comprehensive daytime supervision and enrichment activities for children, providing consistent care and routine throughout the full day.

🏇
Indoor Play & Activities

Engaging games, arts and crafts, storytime, music, and movement activities that stimulate creativity and develop motor skills.

🍇
Meals & Snacks

Nutritious meals and snacks served throughout the day to keep growing children energized and healthy. Contact us for details on our meal program.

👪
Family Communication

Regular updates and open communication with parents to keep families informed and involved in their child's daily experiences and milestones.
