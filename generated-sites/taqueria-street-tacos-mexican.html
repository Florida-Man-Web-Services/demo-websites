Our Story

Real Mexican Street Food in Gainesville

Taqueria Street Tacos brings the vibrant flavors of authentic Mexican street cuisine right to the heart of Gainesville, FL — serving up hand-crafted tacos and traditional dishes made with fresh, quality ingredients every day.

Nestled in northwest Gainesville, we're a neighborhood favorite for locals, students, and families who crave the real thing: bold salsas, tender meats, and homemade tortillas that reflect the true spirit of Mexican cooking.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch or sharing a meal with friends, our welcoming atmosphere and passion for great food make every visit memorable. Contact us for current hours and daily specials.
