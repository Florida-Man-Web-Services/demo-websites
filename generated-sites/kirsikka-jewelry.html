Our Story

Jewelry That Speaks for You

Kirsikka Jewelry is a boutique jewelry design studio serving Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida community. We specialize in handcrafted and custom-designed pieces that reflect individual style, story, and sentiment.

Every piece we create is approached with an artisan's attention to detail, from initial concept through final finish. Whether you are looking for a one-of-a-kind custom creation or a beautifully curated selection of finished jewelry, we bring creativity and craftsmanship to every commission.

We take pride in building lasting relationships with our clients, offering a personalized experience that goes beyond the ordinary jewelry transaction. Contact us to begin your journey toward the perfect piece.
