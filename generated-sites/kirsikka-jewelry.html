01Our Story

Jewelry That Speaks for You

Kirsikka Jewelry is a boutique jewelry design studio serving Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida community. We specialize in handcrafted and custom-designed pieces that reflect individual style, story, and sentiment.

Every piece we create is approached with an artisan's attention to detail, from initial concept through final finish — whether a one-of-a-kind custom creation or a beautifully curated selection of finished jewelry.

We take pride in building lasting relationships with our clients, offering a personalized experience that goes beyond the ordinary. Contact us to begin your journey toward the perfect piece.
