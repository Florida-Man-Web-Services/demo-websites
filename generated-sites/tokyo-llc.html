About Us

A Space Built for Deep Relaxation

Tokyo+ LLC is a massage spa located in the heart of Gainesville, dedicated to providing professional therapeutic and relaxation massage services in a calm, welcoming environment. Whether you are seeking relief from chronic muscle tension, recovering from physical activity, or simply looking for a moment to decompress, our skilled therapists are here to help.

Conveniently situated at 211 SW 4th Ave in downtown Gainesville, we serve residents, students, and visitors throughout the area. We believe that wellness is not a luxury — it is a necessity — and we strive to make quality bodywork accessible to everyone.

What We Offer

Our Services

⚬

Swedish Massage

A classic full-body massage using long, flowing strokes to promote circulation, ease muscle tension, and induce deep relaxation. Contact us for details on session lengths.

⚬

Deep Tissue Massage

Focused pressure targeting deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue — ideal for chronic pain, stiffness, and recovery from physical strain. Contact us for details.

⚬

Hot Stone Massage

Heated basalt stones are used alongside skilled hands to melt away tension and improve circulation, creating a deeply warming and restorative experience.

⚬

Therapeutic / Sports Massage

Designed to address the physical demands of an active lifestyle, this treatment focuses on muscle groups used during exercise to support recovery and performance.

⚬

Couples Massage

Share a relaxing treatment side by side in a shared room, making it a perfect experience for partners, friends, or family. Contact us for availability and details.

⚬

Reflexology

Targeted pressure applied to specific points on the feet, hands, or ears corresponding to other areas of the body — supporting balance and overall well-being.
