About Us

Trusted Dry Cleaning in Gainesville, FL

Quality Cleaners is a full-service dry cleaning establishment serving the northwest Gainesville community with professional garment care. We handle everything from everyday work attire to delicate fabrics, giving each item the attention it deserves.

Conveniently located on NW 43rd Street, we make drop-off and pickup easy for busy residents and students throughout the area. Whether it is a cherished suit, a formal gown, or everyday essentials, our team is committed to returning your clothes looking their best.

For current pricing, turnaround times, and service availability, please contact us directly — we are happy to answer any questions.

"Fresh garments, every time."

Spot and stain removal treatment applied before cleaning to help restore garments affected by stubborn stains.
