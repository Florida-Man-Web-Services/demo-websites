About Us

A Sanctuary for
Natural Hair

Loc'd Down By Toya is a Gainesville-based hair salon dedicated to the art and care of natural hair, with a special focus on locs. We take pride in providing a welcoming, professional environment where clients feel seen, celebrated, and beautifully served.

From first-time loc journeys to mature maintenance, our services are tailored to the health and beauty of your natural texture. We combine technique with intention to help every client's hair thrive.

Rooted in the Gainesville community, we are committed to honoring the cultural significance of locs while delivering quality results that speak for themselves.
