About Us

Soft Washing Done Right in Gainesville

Instant Curb Appeal of Florida specializes in professional soft washing — a low-pressure cleaning method that safely eliminates organic growth, staining, and grime from exterior surfaces without the risk that high-pressure washing can cause.

Based in Gainesville, we understand the unique challenges that Florida's humid climate creates for homeowners. Our biodegradable cleaning solutions are tough on mold, mildew, and algae, yet gentle on your roof, siding, and landscaping.

Whether you want to boost your home's curb appeal before a sale or simply maintain a clean, healthy property, we're here to help. Contact us for details on scheduling and availability.

What We Do

Our Soft Washing Services

We offer a full range of exterior cleaning solutions to keep your property looking its best year-round. Contact us for details on any service.

Roof Soft Washing

Safely remove algae streaks, lichen, and moss from your roof with low-pressure cleaning that extends the life of your shingles.

House Exterior Washing

Restore your siding, stucco, or brick to like-new condition by eliminating dirt, oxidation, and biological growth from your home's exterior.

Driveway & Walkway Cleaning
