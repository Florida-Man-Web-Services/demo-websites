What We Do

Our Painting Services

We offer a comprehensive range of painting solutions for homes and businesses across the Gainesville area. Contact us for details on pricing and availability.

Interior Painting
Full interior paint services including walls, ceilings, trim, doors, and accent features — all with careful prep and clean masking.
Exterior Painting
Weather-resistant exterior coatings for siding, stucco, wood trim, and fascia — protecting your home from Florida's intense sun and humidity.
Commercial Painting
Professional painting for offices, retail spaces, rental properties, and commercial buildings — scheduled to minimize disruption to your operations.
Residential Repaint
Full-home repaints to refresh and modernize your living space, with guidance on color selection and finish options to match your style.
Surface Preparation
Thorough pressure washing, sanding, patching, caulking, and priming to ensure a flawless, long-lasting paint finish on any surface.
Deck & Fence Staining
Professional staining and sealing for wood decks, fences, and outdoor structures to guard against Florida's heat, rain, and UV exposure.
