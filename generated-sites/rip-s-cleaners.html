About Us

Caring for Your Clothes in Gainesville

Rip's Cleaners is a neighborhood dry cleaner serving residents and businesses in downtown Gainesville from our convenient location on North Main Street. We handle everything from everyday garments to delicate fabrics that require careful, expert attention.

Whether you need a suit freshened up for a big meeting or a formal gown prepared for a special occasion, our team treats every item with care and attention to detail. We take pride in returning your clothing clean, pressed, and ready to wear.

Stop by our shop on N Main St or give us a call — we're happy to answer any questions about our garment care process. Contact us for details on turnaround times and available services.
