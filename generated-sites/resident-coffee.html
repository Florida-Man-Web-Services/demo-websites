Our Story

More Than a Cup of Coffee

Resident Coffee is a neighborhood cafe rooted in the Gainesville community, dedicated to sourcing quality beans and serving every drink with care. We believe a great cafe should feel like an extension of home — a place where students, professionals, and locals alike can gather, work, or simply unwind.

From carefully pulled espresso shots to seasonal specialty drinks, our menu reflects a commitment to craft without pretension. Whether you stop in for your morning ritual or settle in for an afternoon, we're glad you're here.

We take pride in being a welcoming space on Gainesville's northwest side, and we look forward to becoming part of your daily routine.
