Our Story

Where Southern Comfort Meets Soul

Wayne Heads Southern Kitchen & Soul is a Gainesville institution committed to serving the rich, hearty flavors of authentic Southern and soul food. From crispy fried chicken to slow-cooked collard greens, every plate is prepared with the care and seasoning that only true Southern cooking can deliver.

Nestled on NE Waldo Road, the restaurant brings neighbors together over food that feels like home — generous portions, familiar recipes, and a warm atmosphere that keeps the Gainesville community coming back time and time again.

Whether you're dropping in for a quick lunch or sitting down for a full Southern spread, you'll find the kind of honest, made-with-love cooking that reminds you good food doesn't need to be complicated.

What We Serve

Soul Food Done Right

Expect generous helpings, bold seasoning, and the honest Southern cooking that Gainesville loves. Contact us for current offerings and daily specials.

Fried Chicken

Hand-seasoned and cooked to crispy, golden perfection — the cornerstone of Southern soul food, made fresh to order.

Collard Greens & Sides

Slow-simmered greens, black-eyed peas, candied yams, and classic Southern sides that complete every plate.

Cornbread & Biscuits

Baked in-house, our cornbread and buttermilk biscuits are the perfect accompaniment to any soul food plate.

Mac & Cheese

Rich, creamy, and baked until bubbly — our mac & cheese is a crowd favorite that pairs with everything on the menu.

Catfish & Seafood

Southern-fried catfish and seasonal seafood offerings — a beloved staple of the soul food tradition, seasoned just right.

Desserts & Sweets

Finish your meal with a slice of sweet potato pie, banana pudding, or another classic Southern dessert. Contact us for today's offerings.
