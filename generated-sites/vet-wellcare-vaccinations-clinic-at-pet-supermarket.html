Vet Wellcare Vaccinations Clinic brings convenient, professional veterinary services to pet owners in Gainesville and the surrounding community. Located conveniently inside Pet Supermarket on NW 13th St, our clinic makes it easy to keep your pet's health a priority while you shop for their everyday needs.

Our licensed veterinary team provides routine wellness exams, core vaccinations, and preventive care tailored to dogs and cats of all ages. We believe every pet deserves quality healthcare without unnecessary hassle or inconvenience for their owners.

Whether you need to update your pet's rabies vaccine, schedule a wellness check, or discuss parasite prevention, our compassionate clinic staff is here to help. Contact us for details on current service availability and scheduling.

What We Offer
Our Veterinary Services

We offer a range of preventive and wellness services to keep your pet healthy. Contact us for details on pricing and scheduling.

Wellness Exams

Routine physical examinations to assess your pet's overall health and catch potential issues early. Contact us for details.

Core Vaccinations

Rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and other essential vaccines to protect your dog or cat from preventable diseases.

Parasite Prevention

Flea, tick, and heartworm prevention guidance and products to keep your pet protected year-round in Florida's climate.

Vaccination Records

Up-to-date health and vaccination documentation to support boarding, grooming, travel, and licensing requirements.

Microchipping

Permanent pet identification implanted quickly and safely, giving you peace of mind if your pet ever gets lost.

Preventive Health Consultations

One-on-one consultations to discuss your pet's diet, lifestyle, and long-term health needs with our veterinary staff.
