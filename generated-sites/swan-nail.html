About Us

A Serene Escape in the Heart of Gainesville

Swan Nail is a full-service nail salon committed to providing high-quality care in a clean, relaxing atmosphere. Whether you're stopping in for a quick polish refresh or a pampering gel treatment, our skilled technicians give every visit the attention it deserves.

Conveniently located on NW 23rd Ave, we serve Gainesville's residents and visitors alike — from students and professionals to families looking for a little self-care. We take pride in maintaining a hygienic, welcoming space where every client feels at ease.

Contact us for current availability, specials, or any questions about our services — we look forward to welcoming you.
