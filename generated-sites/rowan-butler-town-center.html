Our Story

A Trusted Name in Ear Piercing

Rowan Butler Town Center is a dedicated ear piercing studio committed to delivering a clean, comfortable, and professional experience for every guest. We believe that a piercing is more than a procedure — it is a personal expression, and we treat it with the care and attention it deserves.

Our studio uses high-quality, hypoallergenic jewelry and follows strict hygiene protocols so you can feel confident from the moment you sit down. Whether it is your very first piercing or an addition to a curated collection, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Located conveniently at Town Center in Gainesville, we welcome guests of all ages and experience levels. Come in, relax, and leave feeling like yourself — only a little more adorned.
