Our Story

A Neighborhood Bar with Serious Craft

Baby J's Bar is a laid-back yet carefully curated cocktail bar situated in the heart of downtown Gainesville, right on University Avenue. We bring together classic cocktail technique and a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere that keeps locals and visitors coming back.

Whether you're unwinding after a long week, catching up with friends, or discovering something new in your glass, our bar is the kind of place where everyone feels at home. Good drinks, good company — that's what we're about.

Stop in, pull up a stool, and let us take care of the rest.
