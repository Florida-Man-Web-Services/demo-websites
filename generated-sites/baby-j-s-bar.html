The Story

A Neighborhood Bar With Serious Craft

Baby J's Bar sits right on University Avenue in the heart of downtown Gainesville — a cocktail bar that balances genuine craft with a welcoming, no-pretense atmosphere.

Whether you're winding down after a long day, meeting friends for something worth savoring, or catching a DJ set that keeps the night alive, Baby J's is the place locals return to. Good spirits, well-made drinks, and real energy under one roof.

Stop in, grab a stool at the bar, and let us take care of the rest.
