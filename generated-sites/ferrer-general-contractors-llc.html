About Us

Trusted Contractors in Gainesville

FERRER General Contractors LLC is a full-service construction company serving residential and commercial clients throughout Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida region. We bring the same commitment to craftsmanship and reliability to every job, whether it is a ground-up build or a targeted renovation.

Our team coordinates every phase of a project — from initial planning and permitting through final inspection — so clients have a single point of contact and a clear path to completion. We take pride in transparent communication, on-schedule delivery, and work that stands the test of time.

As a locally focused contractor, we understand the specific building codes, soil conditions, and climate considerations that matter in the Gainesville area. Contact us to discuss how we can bring your project to life.
