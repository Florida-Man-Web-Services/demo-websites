Who We Are

A Sanctuary for Your Skin

Gainesville Spa Center by Voupre is a dedicated facial spa serving the Gainesville community, offering a thoughtful range of skin care treatments in a calm and welcoming environment. Our focus is on personalized care — every visit is tailored to your skin's unique needs, whether you're looking to address specific concerns or simply unwind with a rejuvenating facial experience.

Located conveniently on West Newberry Road, we strive to make high-quality facial services accessible to everyone in the area. We use professional-grade products and techniques designed to support healthy, glowing skin for all skin types and tones.

From first-time guests to returning clients, we are committed to providing attentive, professional service in a relaxing space where your comfort always comes first.
