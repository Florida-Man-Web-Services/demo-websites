About Us

Your Trusted Local Shop

Matt K Automotive is a locally operated auto repair shop serving the Gainesville community with dependable, straightforward service on all makes and models. Our technicians bring hands-on expertise to every job, from routine maintenance to more involved mechanical repairs.

We believe in transparent communication — you'll always know what work is being done and why before anything gets started. Whether it's a quick oil change or a complex diagnostic, we treat every vehicle with the same care and attention to detail.

Conveniently located in northeast Gainesville, we're here to keep your car running its best. Contact us for details on scheduling or pricing.

Our Services

Oil & Filter Change

Routine oil and filter service to keep your engine lubricated, clean, and running efficiently for the long haul.

Brake Service
