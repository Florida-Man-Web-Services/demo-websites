About Us

Gainesville's HVAC Professionals

Gainesville Air Conditioning & Heating Services is a dedicated HVAC contractor serving residential and commercial customers throughout Gainesville, FL and the surrounding communities. With Florida's demanding climate, our team is committed to delivering fast, reliable comfort solutions you can depend on year-round.

From sweltering summers to the occasional winter chill, we handle everything from routine tune-ups to full system replacements, helping our customers stay comfortable and energy-efficient no matter the season.

We take pride in our 5-star reputation — built on honest assessments, quality workmanship, and attentive customer care. Contact us for details on pricing, availability, and what we can do for your specific situation.

What We Do

Our HVAC Services

Comprehensive heating and cooling solutions for homes and businesses throughout the Gainesville area.

AC Installation & Replacement
New air conditioning system installation or full unit replacement — properly sized and installed for maximum efficiency in Florida's heat.
AC Repair
Fast diagnosis and repair of air conditioning issues — from refrigerant leaks and compressor problems to fan failures and thermostat malfunctions.
Heating System Services
Installation, repair, and maintenance of heat pumps, furnaces, and other heating systems — keeping your space warm during cooler Florida months.
Preventive Maintenance
Scheduled tune-ups and inspections to keep your HVAC system running at peak performance, extend equipment life, and prevent costly breakdowns.
Indoor Air Quality
Air filtration, purification, and ventilation solutions to improve the air you breathe — important for allergy sufferers and Florida's humid environment.
Duct Cleaning & Sealing
Ductwork inspection, cleaning, and sealing to eliminate airflow inefficiencies, reduce energy waste, and improve system performance throughout your property.
