Motor Specialists

Gainesville's Home for
Electric Motor Repair

Troy's Electric Repair, LLC has built a reputation as one of Gainesville's dedicated electric motor specialists, operating from its Northwest Gainesville facility at 3626 NW 97th Blvd. When industrial motors, pump motors, or commercial equipment fails, Troy's is the call that puts Alachua County businesses back in operation.

The shop specializes in the repair, rewinding, and rebuilding of electric motors across a wide range of sizes and configurations — from single-phase residential units to three-phase industrial workhorses. Generator and alternator service rounds out the core offering, covering standby power systems and portable generators alike.

Conveniently situated in Northwest Gainesville, Troy's serves both businesses and individuals throughout Alachua County looking for professional motor repair without shipping equipment out of the region. Contact us directly for hours and availability.

Services

What We Repair & Restore

Electric Motor Rewinding

Stator and armature rewinding for single-phase and three-phase motors of all sizes — restored to original or improved specification.

Motor Rebuilding & Reconditioning

Full mechanical rebuild: bearing replacement, shaft repair, housing restoration, and dynamic balancing to peak performance.

Generator Service

Winding inspection, repair, and reconditioning of generators for standby systems and industrial generating equipment.

Alternator Repair

Alternator rewinding and reconditioning for a range of applications — industrial through portable power.

Motor Diagnostics & Testing

Electrical and mechanical testing to identify winding failures, bearing wear, insulation breakdown, and common motor faults.

General Electrical Repair

General electrical repair services for commercial and industrial clients throughout the Gainesville area.

Pump & Compressor Motors

Repair of motors used in pump systems, air compressors, HVAC equipment, and other fluid-handling machinery.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled inspection, lubrication, and testing programs to extend motor life and prevent unexpected equipment failure.
