About Us

Reliable Motor Repair
in North Gainesville

Troy's Electric Repair, LLC is a locally owned electric motor repair shop serving the Gainesville area, specializing in the diagnosis, rewinding, and rebuilding of electric motors for a wide range of industrial and commercial applications.

Our technicians work on motors of all sizes — from small single-phase units to large three-phase industrial motors — providing fast turnaround and dependable workmanship that keeps your equipment running efficiently.

Conveniently located on NW 97th Blvd, we are a trusted resource for businesses and individuals throughout Alachua County who need professional motor repair without the hassle of shipping equipment across the state.

What We Do

Our Services

We handle a comprehensive range of electric motor and related repair services. Contact us for details on pricing and turnaround time.

Electric Motor Rewinding

Complete stator and armature rewinding for single-phase and three-phase electric motors of all sizes. Restored to original or improved specifications.

Motor Rebuilding & Reconditioning

Full mechanical rebuilds including bearing replacement, shaft repair, housing restoration, and balancing to bring motors back to peak performance.

Motor Testing & Diagnostics

Comprehensive electrical and mechanical testing to identify winding failures, bearing wear, insulation breakdown, and other common motor faults.

Pump & Compressor Motor Repair

Repair and restoration of motors used in pump systems, air compressors, HVAC equipment, and other fluid-handling machinery commonly found in commercial settings.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled inspection, cleaning, lubrication, and testing programs to extend motor life and prevent unexpected failures in your critical equipment.

Generator & Alternator Service

Winding inspection, repair, and reconditioning of generators and alternators for standby power systems, portable generators, and industrial generating equipment.
