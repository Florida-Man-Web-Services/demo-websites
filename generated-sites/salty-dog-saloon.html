About Us

A True Gainesville Saloon

Salty Dog Saloon is a beloved dive bar in the heart of Gainesville, FL, sitting right on West University Avenue where the Gator spirit runs deep. Whether you are winding down after a long day or gearing up for a big game, this is the kind of place that feels like home the moment you walk through the door.

We keep things unpretentious — great drinks, friendly faces, and a laid-back atmosphere that locals have come to rely on. From students to longtime residents, everyone finds their place at the Salty Dog.
