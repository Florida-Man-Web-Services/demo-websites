Who We Are

Northwood Cooling Heating & Appliance Services

Northwood Cooling Heating & Appliance Services Inc is a trusted HVAC and appliance service provider serving the Gainesville area and surrounding communities throughout North Central Florida. We specialize in keeping homes and businesses comfortable year-round with reliable heating, cooling, and appliance solutions tailored to Florida's unique climate demands.

Our team of experienced technicians is committed to prompt, professional service — whether you need routine maintenance, an emergency repair, or a full system replacement. We work with all major brands and equipment types to ensure every job is done right the first time.

What We Do

Our Services

We handle the full range of HVAC and home appliance needs — from quick tune-ups to complete system installs.

AC Installation & Replacement

Full installation and replacement of central air conditioning systems, ductless mini-splits, and packaged units sized right for your space.

Air Conditioning Repair

Diagnosis and repair of all AC problems including refrigerant leaks, compressor issues, electrical faults, and poor cooling performance.

Heating System Services

Installation, repair, and maintenance of heat pumps, electric furnaces, and gas heating systems to keep you warm through Florida's cooler months.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled tune-ups and seasonal maintenance plans that extend equipment life, improve efficiency, and catch small issues before they become costly repairs.

Indoor Air Quality

Air filtration, UV purifiers, humidity control, and duct cleaning solutions to improve the air you breathe inside your home or business.

Appliance Repair & Service

Service and repair of major household appliances. Contact us for details on the makes, models, and appliance types we currently service.
