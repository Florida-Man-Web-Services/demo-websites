About Us

Rooted in Gainesville

Square Yard Landscaping provides dependable, professional lawn care and landscaping services tailored to the unique climate and soil conditions of North Central Florida. Our team understands what it takes to keep local properties healthy and looking their best through Gainesville's hot summers and mild winters.

From routine lawn maintenance to complete landscape enhancements, we bring attention to detail and a genuine care for the environment to every job. We serve residential and commercial properties throughout the Gainesville area.
