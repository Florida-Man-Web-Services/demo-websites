About Us

Your Local HVAC Solution

Richardson's Heating and Air Conditioning is a trusted heating equipment supplier and service provider serving the Gainesville, FL area. We specialize in keeping homes and businesses comfortable through Florida's hot summers and cooler winters with reliable HVAC equipment and expert support.

Whether you need a new system installed, an existing unit serviced, or guidance on the right heating and cooling solution for your space, our team is here to help. We carry quality equipment suited for the demands of North Central Florida's climate.

Contact us today to discuss your heating and air conditioning needs — we're committed to delivering dependable comfort solutions at a professional standard.
