About

A self service wash, on your time

Brighton Beach Car Wash gives Gainesville drivers a hands-on, self service setup — pull in, grab the wand, and take care of your vehicle exactly the way you like it. It's a straightforward, come-as-you-are spot for a quick rinse or a thorough scrub.

Located on NW US Hwy 441, it's an easy stop whether you're heading out of town or just want a fresh-looking ride. Reach out anytime for bay availability and details.

How It Works

Four easy steps

A simple self service flow that puts you in control of the clean.

STEP 01

Foam & Pre-Soak

Start with a foam pass to help loosen dirt and road grime before you scrub.

STEP 02

Wash & Rinse

Use the wand to wash the full exterior, then rinse it all away at your own pace.

STEP 03

Wheels & Tires

Target the wheels and tires to clear brake dust and buildup for a complete finish.

STEP 04

Spot-Free Finish

Wrap up with a final rinse and drive off with a bright, freshly washed ride.
