About Us

Clean Cars,
Your Way

Brighton Beach Car Wash offers residents and commuters throughout the Gainesville area a convenient self service facility designed to put the control in your hands. With easy-to-use bays and on-site equipment, you can give your vehicle the thorough wash it deserves on your own schedule.

We are conveniently located on NW US Hwy 441, making us an easy stop whether you're heading into town or out toward the county. Our bays are kept clean and well-maintained so you always have the tools you need for a great result.

Whether you're cleaning off everyday road grime or giving your ride a full rinse-and-shine treatment, Brighton Beach Car Wash keeps it simple and affordable. Contact us for current bay availability and pricing details.

Our Services

Everything you need to keep your vehicle looking its best — contact us for current pricing and bay availability.

High-Pressure Wash

Powerful spray removes road dirt, mud, and grime from your vehicle's body, wheels, and undercarriage.

Foam Brush Scrub

Self service foam brush lets you scrub every panel and surface by hand for a thorough, hands-on clean.

Pre-Soak Rinse
