Our Story

Body Art as a Personal Statement

Emerald Scarab Tattoo Parlor is a dedicated studio offering custom tattoo work and professional body piercing in a clean, welcoming environment. We believe that every piece of body art is a personal statement, and we work closely with each client to bring their vision to life with care and skill.

Our studio is committed to the highest standards of safety and hygiene, using quality inks, sterile equipment, and industry-best practices for every appointment. Whether you are getting your first tattoo or your fifteenth, you can expect a comfortable and respectful experience from the moment you walk in.
