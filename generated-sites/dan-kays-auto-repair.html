About Us

A Shop That Treats You Right

Dan Kays Auto Repair is a full-service auto repair shop located in the heart of Gainesville, FL, committed to providing honest, high-quality mechanical work for all makes and models. We pride ourselves on transparent communication — you'll always know what needs to be done and why before any work begins.

Conveniently situated on SE 4th Street, our shop serves drivers throughout Gainesville and the surrounding area with the kind of straightforward, dependable service that keeps customers coming back. Whether it's a routine maintenance visit or a more complex repair, we treat every vehicle with the same level of care and attention to detail.

Our team uses up-to-date diagnostic equipment and quality parts so that every repair is done right the first time. Contact us for details on any specific service or to schedule your next appointment.
