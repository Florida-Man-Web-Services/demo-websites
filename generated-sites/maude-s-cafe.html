Our Story

A Gathering Place for the Gainesville Community

Maude's Cafe is a beloved independent coffee shop tucked into the vibrant downtown Gainesville neighborhood, offering a relaxed, welcoming space for students, locals, and visitors alike.

We take pride in crafting quality espresso drinks and serving a rotating selection of house-made treats, all in an atmosphere that feels like a home away from home.

Whether you're catching up with friends, powering through a work session, or simply savoring a quiet cup, Maude's is your spot. Contact us for current hours and specials.
