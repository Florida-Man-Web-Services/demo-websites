About Us

Your Trusted
Local Handyperson

Honey & Hearth Professional Services is a locally owned handyperson business serving homeowners and renters throughout Gainesville and the surrounding Alachua County area. We handle the repairs and improvements that keep your home running smoothly and looking its best.

We take pride in arriving on time, communicating clearly, and leaving every job site cleaner than we found it. No project is too small, and we bring the same level of care to a leaky faucet as we do to a full room renovation.

Whether you need a quick fix or a multi-day project completed, contact us to discuss your needs and we will work with your schedule and budget.

Our Services

We handle a wide variety of home repair and improvement tasks. Contact us for details on any service or to describe a project not listed here.

🔨

General Carpentry & Repairs

Door and window adjustments, trim work, shelving installation, cabinet repairs, and general wood repairs throughout your home.

🚿

Plumbing Fixture Repairs

Faucet and toilet repairs, showerhead replacement, minor leak fixes, and other household plumbing tasks that don't require a licensed plumber.

💡

Minor Electrical Tasks

Outlet and light switch replacement, ceiling fan installation, light fixture swaps, and similar small electrical jobs within handyperson scope.

Weather stripping, gutter cleaning, caulk replacement, screen repair, and the miscellaneous tasks that keep your home in good shape year-round.
