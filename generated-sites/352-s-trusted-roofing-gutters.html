About Us

Gainesville's Roofing Specialists

352's Trusted Roofing & Gutters is a locally owned contractor serving Gainesville and the surrounding Alachua County area. We specialize in full roof replacements, storm damage repairs, and complete gutter system installations for both residential and commercial properties.

Florida's climate demands roofing solutions that stand up to intense heat, heavy rain, and severe storms. Our team uses quality materials and time-tested techniques to ensure every project is completed to last — protecting your home or business for years to come.

From minor leak repairs to brand-new roof systems, we provide honest assessments and workmanship you can count on. Contact us for details on any of our services.

What We Do

Our Roofing & Gutter Services

From routine maintenance to complete roof replacements, we offer a full range of services to keep your property protected. Contact us for details on pricing and availability.

Roof Replacement

Complete tear-off and new roof installation using durable shingles, tile, or metal systems suited for Florida's climate. Contact us for details.

Roof Repair

From missing shingles to storm damage and active leaks, we diagnose and repair issues quickly to prevent further damage to your property.

Gutter Installation

Properly installed gutters protect your foundation and landscaping. We offer seamless gutter systems sized for your specific roof and drainage needs.

Gutter Cleaning & Repair

Clogged or sagging gutters can cause serious water damage. We provide thorough cleaning, re-pitching, resealing, and section repairs. Contact us for details.

Storm Damage Assessment

After a major storm, let us inspect your roof for hidden damage. We provide thorough assessments and can assist with the insurance documentation process.

Roof Inspections & Maintenance

Routine inspections catch small issues before they become costly problems. We offer preventative maintenance plans to extend the life of your roof.
