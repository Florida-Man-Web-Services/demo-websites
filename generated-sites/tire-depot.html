Who We Are

Your Local Tire Specialists

Tire Depot is a used tire shop serving the Gainesville community from our location on North Main Street. We carry a wide selection of quality pre-owned tires in all common sizes, making it easy to find what your vehicle needs without breaking the bank.

Whether you need a single replacement or a full set, our team works quickly to get you back on the road safely. We take pride in offering reliable tires at prices that fit real budgets.

Stop by or give us a call — we are happy to help you find the right fit for your car, truck, or SUV.
