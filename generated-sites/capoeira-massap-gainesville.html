About Us

Authentic Capoeira in the Heart of Gainesville

Capoeira Massapê Gainesville is a dedicated academy bringing the vibrant art of capoeira — Brazil's unique blend of martial arts, acrobatics, and music — to the Gainesville community. Our classes are open to students of all backgrounds and experience levels, from curious beginners to seasoned practitioners.

Training at Massapê means more than learning kicks and dodges; it means immersing yourself in the music, the Portuguese vocabulary, the rich Afro-Brazilian history, and the deep sense of camaraderie that capoeira inspires.

Whether you're seeking a creative way to get fit, develop self-defense awareness, or connect with a welcoming community, our school offers a safe and energetic environment to grow. Contact us for details on current schedules and enrollment.

What We Offer

Classes & Programs

We offer structured programs for every stage of the capoeira journey. Contact us for current schedules, class times, and registration information.

🏃

Beginner Capoeira Classes

Start your journey with foundational movements, the ginga, basic kicks, and an introduction to the music and culture that make capoeira unique.

🏼

Intermediate & Advanced Training

Deepen your game with advanced techniques, complex sequences, play strategy, and preparation for batizado events and belt progressions.

🏂

Kids Capoeira Program

A fun, structured program designed to build coordination, discipline, and confidence in younger students through the playful art of capoeira.

🎹

Music & Instruments

Learn to play the berimbau, atabaque, and pandeiro — the instruments that form the heartbeat of the capoeira roda and the living tradition behind it.

🏁

Open Roda Sessions

Practice your game in an authentic roda setting alongside fellow students and instructors. A core part of capoeira development and community.

🌟

Workshops & Special Events

Periodic intensives, guest instructor workshops, and cultural events celebrating Afro-Brazilian art, history, and capoeira traditions. Contact us for upcoming dates.
