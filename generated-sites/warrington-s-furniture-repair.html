About Us

Trusted Local Furniture Restoration

Warrington's Furniture Repair is a locally owned shop in Gainesville, FL, dedicated to breathing new life into worn, damaged, or outdated furniture. We take pride in combining traditional woodworking techniques with modern finishing methods to achieve lasting, beautiful results.

Whether you have a family heirloom that needs careful attention or everyday pieces that could use a fresh look, our team approaches every project with the same care and craftsmanship.

We serve residential and commercial clients throughout the Gainesville area. Contact us to discuss your piece and get an assessment.

What We Do

Our Services

🪵

Wood Repair & Restoration

Fixing cracks, splits, warps, and structural damage to restore the integrity and beauty of wooden furniture.

🎨

Refinishing & Staining

Stripping old finishes and applying fresh stains, paints, or lacquers to match your desired look and protect the wood.

🧵

Upholstery Repair

Repairing torn, worn, or sagging upholstery on chairs, sofas, and other upholstered pieces — contact us for details.

🔩

Structural & Joint Repair

Re-gluing loose joints, repairing broken legs, and reinforcing frames to make furniture sturdy and safe again.

🏛️

Antique & Heirloom Restoration

Careful, respectful restoration of antiques and family heirlooms with attention to preserving their original character.

✨

Touch-Up & Minor Repairs

Scratches, dents, chips, water rings — we handle smaller cosmetic repairs that make a big difference in appearance.
