About UsNo. 01

Trusted Local Furniture Restoration

Warrington's Furniture Repair is a locally owned shop in Gainesville, FL, dedicated to breathing new life into worn, damaged, or outdated furniture. We take pride in combining traditional woodworking techniques with modern finishing methods to achieve lasting, beautiful results.

Whether you have a family heirloom that needs careful attention or everyday pieces that could use a fresh look, our team approaches every project with the same care and craftsmanship. We serve residential and commercial clients throughout the Gainesville area. Contact us to discuss your piece and get an assessment.
