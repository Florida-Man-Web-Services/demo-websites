About Us

Craftsmanship You Can See

Joe Lawton Custom Paint is a trusted auto body and custom paint shop serving drivers throughout Gainesville and the surrounding North Florida area. From minor collision repairs to full custom paint jobs, the shop applies professional-grade techniques to make vehicles look their best.

Located on S Main St, the shop focuses on quality workmanship and attention to detail, using industry-standard materials and equipment to deliver finishes that last. Whether you need factory-match color correction or a one-of-a-kind custom look, this is the place locals turn to.

What We Do

Our Services

From collision repair to custom finishes, we handle a wide range of auto body and paint work. Contact us for details on pricing and availability.

🌟

Custom Paint Jobs

Full vehicle custom paint in your choice of color, finish, and style — from subtle factory-match to bold custom looks built to stand out.

🔨

Collision & Body Repair

Structural and cosmetic repair following accidents or impacts, restoring your vehicle's body panels and frame to pre-collision condition.

🚗

Paint Correction & Touch-Up

Professional color-matching and spot repair for scratches, chips, and faded paint areas — blended seamlessly with the surrounding finish.

⚡

Dent & Ding Removal

Targeted repair of minor dents and dings using proven bodywork methods to restore smooth, factory-look surfaces.

🔄

Primer & Surface Prep

Thorough surface preparation including sanding, priming, and sealing to ensure paint adhesion and a flawless, long-lasting finish.

✨

Clear Coat & Finishing

Application of high-quality clear coat and polish to protect your new paint and give it a deep, showroom-quality gloss.
