The Shop
Finishes that make heads turn.

Joe Lawton Custom Paint is a trusted auto body and custom paint shop serving drivers throughout Gainesville and the surrounding North Florida area. From minor collision repairs to full custom paint jobs, the shop applies professional-grade techniques to make vehicles look their best.

Located on S Main St, the shop focuses on quality workmanship and attention to detail, using industry-standard materials and equipment to deliver finishes that last. Whether you need factory-match color correction or a one-of-a-kind custom look, this is the place locals turn to.
