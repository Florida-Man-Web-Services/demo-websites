Who We Are

Crafted with Care

Only Nails is a full-service nail salon serving the Gainesville community, dedicated to delivering high-quality nail care in a welcoming and relaxing environment. From classic manicures to intricate nail art, every visit is tailored to help you look and feel your best.

Our skilled technicians stay current with the latest trends and techniques, using professional-grade products to ensure lasting results. Whether you are treating yourself or preparing for a special occasion, we take pride in the precision and artistry behind every service.

Conveniently located on SW Archer Road, Only Nails is your go-to destination for polished, professional nail care right here in Gainesville. Walk-ins are welcome and appointments are encouraged.
