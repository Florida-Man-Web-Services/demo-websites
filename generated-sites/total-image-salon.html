Our Story

A Salon Built on Craft & Care

Total Image Salon has been a trusted destination in downtown Gainesville for clients who want expert hair care in a welcoming, professional environment. Nestled in the heart of NW 3rd Street, our stylists take pride in delivering personalized results that suit your unique look and lifestyle.

From precision cuts and color transformations to relaxing treatments, we approach every appointment with attentive skill and genuine care. Whether you're a longtime local or visiting for the first time, you'll leave feeling refreshed and confident.
