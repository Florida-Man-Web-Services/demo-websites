About Us

A Local Shop You Can Trust

Mallard's Upholstery is a Gainesville-based shop dedicated to delivering skilled, attentive craftsmanship on every project that comes through the door. Whether it's a cherished piece of furniture or a vehicle interior in need of attention, we treat each job with the care it deserves.

We work with a wide variety of fabrics, vinyls, and leathers to match your style and budget. Our hands-on approach means you get personalized service and results built to last. Serving Gainesville and the surrounding North Central Florida area — contact us to discuss your project.
