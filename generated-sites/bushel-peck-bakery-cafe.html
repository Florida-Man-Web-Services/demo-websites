Our Story

A Neighborhood Gathering Place

Bushel & Peck is a beloved bakery and cafe nestled in the heart of Gainesville, crafting fresh-baked goods and specialty coffee drinks for the local community. Every item that comes out of our kitchen is made with quality ingredients and a genuine love for the craft of baking.

Whether you’re stopping in for a morning pastry on your way to work or settling in for an afternoon with a warm drink, we believe in creating a space where everyone feels at home. Gainesville is our community, and every visit matters to us.

"Good food, good coffee, good people."
