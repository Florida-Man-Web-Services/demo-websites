About Us

A Salon Built Around You

P.First is a full-service hair salon serving the Gainesville community with personalized care and professional technique. Our focus is simple: every client leaves feeling confident and looking their best, no matter the occasion.

We take time to listen, consult, and tailor each service to your hair type, lifestyle, and goals. From everyday cuts to transformative color, we approach each chair with the same dedication to quality and detail.

Located on NE 10th Street, P.First is a welcoming space for anyone looking for skilled stylists and a comfortable salon experience right here in Gainesville.
