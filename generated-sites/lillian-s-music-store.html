Our Story

A Live Music Bar in the Heart of Gainesville

Lillian's Music Store is a beloved fixture of Gainesville's vibrant downtown scene, serving as a gathering place for locals and visitors who share a passion for live music and good drinks. Nestled on SE 1st St, our stage has hosted an eclectic mix of local talent and touring acts spanning every genre imaginable.

Whether you're unwinding after a long day or dancing into the early hours, Lillian's offers a warm, intimate atmosphere that keeps people coming back. We take pride in being a true community bar where the music is always the main event.
