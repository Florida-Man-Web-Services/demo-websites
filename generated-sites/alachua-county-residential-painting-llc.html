About Us

Dedicated to Quality Painting in Gainesville

Alachua County Residential Painting LLC is a locally owned painting company serving homeowners throughout Gainesville and the surrounding Alachua County area. We take pride in delivering meticulous surface preparation and clean, lasting finishes that hold up to Florida's demanding climate.

From refreshing a single room to repainting an entire home's exterior, our crew approaches every project with the same attention to detail — protecting your property, using premium paints, and leaving your space spotless when we're done.

We serve customers across Gainesville and nearby communities, and our 5-star Google rating reflects our commitment to honest communication and craftsmanship you can count on.

What We Offer

Our Painting Services

Interior Painting

Full interior room painting including walls, ceilings, trim, and doors. We protect furniture and flooring and leave every space clean and refreshed.

Exterior Painting

Full exterior house painting designed to withstand Florida's humidity, UV exposure, and seasonal rain. Proper priming and weatherproof topcoats included.

Cabinet & Trim Painting

Precision painting for kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities, baseboards, crown molding, and window trim for a crisp, factory-quality finish.

Pressure Washing & Surface Prep

Thorough cleaning, sanding, caulking, and priming to ensure paint adheres properly and the finished result looks its best and lasts for years.

Color Consultation

Guidance on color selection to complement your home's architecture, landscaping, and neighborhood — contact us for details on how we can help.

Touch-Ups & Repairs

Spot repairs for chipped, peeling, or water-damaged paint, including minor drywall patching before repainting to restore a smooth, seamless appearance.
