About Us

Residential painting is all we do.

Alachua County Residential Painting LLC is a locally owned company focused on homes — interiors, exteriors, trim and touch-ups. We protect your furniture and flooring, keep our lines clean, and leave every space refreshed and tidy.

We take pride in careful prep and materials chosen to hold up to Florida's climate, so your paint looks great long after we've packed up. Contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your home.

What We Do

Our Services

Interior Painting

Full interior room painting including walls, ceilings, trim and doors. We protect furniture and flooring and leave every space clean and refreshed.

Exterior Painting

Weather-ready exterior coatings with thorough surface prep, built to stand up to Florida heat, humidity and sun.

Trim & Doors

Precise coatings for baseboards, doors and trim — the finishing details that pull a whole room together.

Paint Repair & Touch-Ups

Spot repairs for chipped, peeling or water-damaged paint, including minor drywall patching before repainting to restore a smooth, seamless appearance.
