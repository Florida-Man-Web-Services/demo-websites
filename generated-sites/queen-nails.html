Our Story

A Sanctuary of Style & Self-Care

Queen Nails is a full-service nail salon in northwest Gainesville, dedicated to making every client feel pampered and confident from the moment they walk in. Our skilled technicians bring precision and artistry to every manicure, pedicure, and nail enhancement, using quality products in a clean, welcoming environment.

Whether you are treating yourself to a quick polish refresh or a luxurious spa pedicure, our team is committed to delivering results you will love. We proudly serve the Gainesville community with attentive, personalized care for every visit.
