About Us

A Center Built for This Community

The Rosa B Williams Center is a community recreation facility in northwest Gainesville dedicated to enriching the lives of residents through accessible fitness, programming, and social activities. We serve families, seniors, youth, and individuals looking for a welcoming place to stay active and engaged. Located on NW 1st Street, our center is a cornerstone of neighborhood life and a gathering point for people of all backgrounds and abilities.
