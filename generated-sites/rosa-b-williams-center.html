The Front Page

A center built for this community

The Rosa B Williams Center is a community recreation facility in northwest Gainesville dedicated to enriching the lives of residents through accessible fitness, programming, and social activities.

We serve families, seniors, youth, and individuals looking for a welcoming place to stay active and engaged. Located on NW 1st Street, our center is a cornerstone of neighborhood life.

It is a gathering point for people of all backgrounds and abilities — where fitness and recreation sit at the heart of everything we do, and every resident is welcome through the door.
