About Us

Quality Upholstery at the Heart of Gainesville

Big Sun Upholstery & Fabrics is a trusted local shop dedicated to bringing new life to furniture, automotive interiors, and custom fabric projects throughout the Gainesville area. Our skilled team works with a wide selection of quality materials to match every style and budget.

Whether you have a cherished heirloom sofa in need of restoration or a boat seat ready for a fresh look, we treat every project with the same attention to detail and pride in workmanship that our customers have come to rely on.
