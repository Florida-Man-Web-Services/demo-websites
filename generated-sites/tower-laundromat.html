About Us

A Clean, Convenient Laundry Experience

Tower Laundromat provides Gainesville residents with a comfortable, well-maintained facility to take care of all their laundry needs quickly and efficiently. Our spacious layout fits everything from everyday loads to bulky items like comforters and blankets that won't fit in a standard home washer.

We keep our machines in top condition so you spend less time waiting and more time getting on with your day. Whether you are a UF student, a busy family, or anyone in between, Tower Laundromat makes laundry day simpler.

Located at 600 NW 75th St in northwest Gainesville, we are easy to reach and ready to help with all your washing and drying needs. Contact us for current details on availability and hours.
