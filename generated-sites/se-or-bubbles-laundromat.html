About Us

Your go-to spot for fresh, clean laundry

Señor Bubbles Laundromat is a convenient, well-maintained facility serving the Gainesville community with reliable washing and drying machines in a comfortable environment. Whether you're a student, a busy family, or just need a quick wash, we have the capacity and equipment to get the job done right. Stop in at our SW 24th Ave location and experience the ease of getting your laundry taken care of all in one place.
