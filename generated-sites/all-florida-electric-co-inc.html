What We Do

Electrical Services

From small repairs to large installations, we handle a wide range of electrical needs for residential and commercial clients.

Residential Electrical Services

Wiring, outlets, switches, lighting, and general electrical repairs for homes and apartments throughout the Gainesville area.

Commercial Electrical Services

Dependable electrical solutions for offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and other commercial properties — installed to code.
