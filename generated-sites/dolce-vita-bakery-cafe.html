Our Story

A Corner of Italy
in Gainesville

Dolce Vita Bakery Cafe brings the warmth and craft of an Italian-inspired patisserie to the heart of Gainesville, serving the community with house-made pastries, artisan breads, and specialty coffee drinks. Every item that leaves our kitchen is prepared with quality ingredients and a dedication to old-world technique.

Whether you stop in for your morning espresso and a flaky croissant or linger over a slice of cake in the afternoon, Dolce Vita is a place to slow down, savor, and enjoy the sweeter side of life.

We are proud to be part of the northwest Gainesville neighborhood and welcome locals, UF families, and visitors alike.
