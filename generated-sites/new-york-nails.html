About Us

Thoughtful Care for Your Nails

New York Nails is a full-service nail salon conveniently located in southwest Gainesville, dedicated to delivering polished results in a clean, welcoming environment.

Our skilled technicians take pride in precise, attentive work — whether you're looking for a classic manicure or an elaborate nail design, we treat every client with care and professionalism.

Serving the Gainesville community, we are committed to maintaining high standards of hygiene, using quality products, and ensuring every guest leaves feeling refreshed and confident.
