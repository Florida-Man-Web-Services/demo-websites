A gathering place for the Gainesville community

Grove Street Farmers Market is a vibrant community hub where North Central Florida farmers, artisans, and food makers come together to offer the freshest seasonal produce, handcrafted goods, and locally sourced products.

Nestled in the heart of Gainesville, the market brings neighbors together and supports the region's hardworking growers and makers, making it easy to eat well and shop local all in one place.

Whether you're stocking up on seasonal vegetables, picking up fresh baked goods, or discovering something new, Grove Street Farmers Market offers a welcoming, open-air experience that keeps the community connected to its food and its roots.
