About Us

Serving the Gainesville Community

Wash King is a convenient, full-service laundromat located in Gainesville, FL, providing residents with clean and reliable self-service washing and drying facilities. Our location on NE 16th Ave is easily accessible and equipped with a range of machine sizes to handle any load — from everyday clothes to bulky comforters and bedding.

We are committed to keeping our facility clean, safe, and comfortable so you can get in, get your laundry done, and get on with your day. Whether you visit us weekly or just need a one-time solution, Wash King is here to make laundry day simpler.

Centrally located in northeast Gainesville, we serve UF students, families, and local residents looking for an efficient and straightforward laundry experience. Contact us for details on current hours and availability.
