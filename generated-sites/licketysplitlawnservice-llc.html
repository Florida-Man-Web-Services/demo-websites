About Us

Lawn Care You Can Count On

LicketySplit Lawn Service LLC is a locally owned lawn care company serving homeowners and businesses throughout the Gainesville area. We take pride in delivering dependable, high-quality results that keep outdoor spaces healthy, tidy, and looking their best through every season.

Whether you need routine maintenance or a one-time cleanup, our team arrives promptly, works efficiently, and leaves your property in excellent condition. We understand the unique climate and turf needs of North Central Florida and bring that knowledge to every job.

Reach out today to discuss how we can help you maintain a yard you are proud of — contact us for details on scheduling and availability.
