About Us

Painting You Can Be Proud Of

Sunshine Professional Painters is a trusted painting contractor serving Gainesville, FL and the surrounding communities, bringing fresh color and renewed life to homes and commercial properties of all sizes.

Our team takes pride in meticulous surface preparation, precise application, and thorough cleanup — ensuring every project looks beautiful and holds up to Florida's demanding climate for years to come.

Whether you need a single accent wall refreshed or an entire exterior repainted, we approach every job with the same dedication to quality and clear communication with our customers.

What We Offer

Our Painting Services

From prep work to the final coat, we handle residential and commercial painting projects with care and expertise.

Interior Painting

Full interior painting for rooms, ceilings, trim, and doors — with careful prep and clean results that transform any living or work space.

Exterior Painting

Weather-resistant exterior coatings for siding, trim, stucco, and more — protecting your property from sun, humidity, and rain.

Commercial Painting

Professional painting for offices, retail spaces, and commercial buildings — completed efficiently to minimize disruption to your business.

Cabinet & Trim Painting

Detailed painting and refinishing of kitchen cabinets, baseboards, crown molding, and other trim work for a polished finished look.

Pressure Washing

Surface cleaning and preparation using pressure washing before painting to ensure proper adhesion and a long-lasting, smooth finish.

Color Consultation

Guidance on color selection and finish options to help you find the right look for your interior or exterior — at no added hassle.
