Small batches. Real hands. A neighborhood bakery on Gainesville's southwest side.

BakerBaker is a neighborhood artisan bakery tucked into Gainesville's vibrant southwest side, bringing handcrafted breads, pastries, and sweets to the local community every day.

Every item that leaves our kitchen is made in small batches using quality ingredients, time-honored techniques, and a genuine passion for the craft of baking.

Whether you're stopping in for a morning treat or planning a special order for an event, we're here to make it delicious. Contact us for details on custom orders and availability.
