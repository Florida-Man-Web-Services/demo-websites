# Phone Script — Website Outreach

Use for businesses that have a phone number listed. Keep it under 60 seconds.
Adjust the opener based on who answers (owner vs. staff).

---

## When staff answers

> "Hi, my name is Noah — I'm a local web developer here in Gainesville.
> Could I speak with the owner or manager for just a moment?
> It's about their online presence — nothing urgent, I just have something
> I'd like to show them."

*[If they ask what it's about before transferring:]*

> "I noticed [BUSINESS NAME] doesn't have a website, so I went ahead and
> built a free demo — I just want to make sure the right person sees it."

---

## When owner/decision-maker answers

> "Hi, is this the owner of [BUSINESS NAME]?
>
> Great — my name is Noah, I'm a web developer based here in Gainesville.
> I'm calling because I noticed your business doesn't have a website, and
> I actually already built one for you — completely free.
>
> You can pull it up right now at:
> florida-man-bioscience.github.io/demo-websites/generated-sites/[SLUG].html
>
> It's already got your name, your address, [and your phone number], and it
> looks professional on mobile and desktop. There's no cost to look at it.
>
> If you decide you like it and want to go live — meaning your own domain
> name and customers can find you on Google — I handle all of that. But
> there's zero pressure. I just figured you'd want to see it.
>
> Do you have a way to pull that up right now, or would it be easier if I
> texted you the link?"

---

## If they say "send it to my email"

> "Absolutely — what's the best email for you?
> I'll send it over right now with all the details."

*[Send the cold email template immediately after the call.]*

---

## If they say "I'm not interested"

> "Totally understand — the demo is yours to keep either way, so if you
> ever change your mind the link will still be there.
> Have a good one."

---

## If they say "How much does it cost?"

> "The demo is completely free — I built it on my own time.
> If you want to take it live with a real domain name and have Google
> start finding you, I charge a flat rate to finalize and host everything.
> Happy to walk you through exactly what's included when you've had a
> chance to look at the demo. No surprises."

---

## Voicemail

> "Hi, this message is for the owner of [BUSINESS NAME]. My name is Noah —
> I'm a web developer in Gainesville. I noticed your business doesn't have
> a website so I went ahead and built a free one for you. You can see it at
> florida-man-bioscience.github.io/demo-websites/generated-sites/[SLUG].html
> — that's all one word. If you want to go live, I can make it happen fast.
> My number is [YOUR PHONE]. Thanks."
